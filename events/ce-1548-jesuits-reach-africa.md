# Jesuits reach Africa

The Jesuit expansion slowly continues south
to [Africa](https://www.creighton.edu/sites/default/files/2022-01/35-Africa.pdf)
and establishes colonies in an attempt to assimilate indigenous religions of Ethiopia, Egypt, the area of the Zambesi
River, Angola and Cape Verde.


![Kazan kingdom / Khazar Khaganate - center of Judaic religion](https://clevertree.github.io/paradigm-threat-files/history/chronology/tartaria/map_khazar_khagnate.jpg?w=384)
*In the middle of the XVI century the Kazan kingdom – aka the legendary 'ancient' Khazar Khaganate – becomes the centre of the Judaic religion. The Kazan czar, i.e. The Khazar Kagan and his court convert to Judaism. Kazan makes an attempt to break away from the Empire.*
