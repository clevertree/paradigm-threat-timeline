# Earth leaves last Absu layer and Jupiter consumes Venus again

This new period of stability lasted 156 years, ending abruptly when the nonlinear configuration lost its last Absu layer. Exposed to the Sun's electromagnetic field, each planet formed its own [magnetosphere](http://www.plasmacosmology.net/tech.html). Seasons began on Earth for the first time. The formation of deserts forced populations of Western Asia, the Indian Subcontinent, and North Africa to migrate. The Egyptian and [Akkadian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akkadian_Empire) kingdoms collapsed. Earth remained in nonlinear orbit with Jupiter, and the year extended to 273 days. It would be another 200 years before Egypt repopulated, beginning the [Middle Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_Kingdom_of_Egypt). As Venus became exposed to the Sun's field, it retreated into the safety of Jupiter's magnetosphere, once again _consumed into the Gas Giant's interior_.

## 2167 BCE — Jupiter Catches on Fire

Within 25 years, Gas-Giant Jupiter — still appearing on its coma-tail mountain, having recently consumed Venus — became fully exposed to the Sun's field and caught on fire for 20 straight years. Humans world-wide remembered this spectacle as [The Burning Bush](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burning_bush) (Mosaic), [Chilam Balam](https://www.sacred-texts.com/nam/maya/cbc/cbc15.htm) (Mayan), and many others. The Old Testament story of [Moses](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moses) redacts the planet saviour Jupiter (Zeus) into a mythical human prophet "Mo-Zeus" representing a '[Universal Creator and Saviour God](https://sites.google.com/a/kent.edu/jwattles/home/comparative-religious-thought/judaism/moses-concept-of-god)'.

Under the gas giant's changing influence, Earth underwent further cataclysm. In Hebrew and Egyptian tradition, the Burning Bush was a sign that God (Jupiter) was unhappy with His creation (Humanity). The Earth groaned, the ground vibrated, and a trumpeting sound was heard and _felt_ by every creature up to [613](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/613_commandments) times. Humans interpreted each exultation as a new Commandment spoken directly by God. This became the basis of the 10 Commandments of the [Mosaic Covenant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mosaic_covenant). For Jupiter's full mythological role, see [Jupiter replaces Saturn as the new Saviour](/timeline?event=evt-jupiter-replaces-saturn-as-the-new-saviour-aka-zeu).

This event symbolizes the end of King Jupiter's reign and a changing of the 'rules'. To most humans, Jupiter's epic display meant the _powers that be_ did not represent any gods. The **Pyramidal Empire** reacted by creating the [Abrahamic Covenant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Covenant_of_the_pieces) — replacing direct rulership with indirect religious mind-control. In Genesis 22, the [Binding of Isaac](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Binding_of_Isaac) describes these new rules. Abraham was originally Abram (containing 'Ram'). God ordered Abram to sacrifice his son Isaac as a test of loyalty. He complied until stopped by emissaries of the age of Aries (the Ram), who informed him he could slaughter a ram instead. Going forward, subjects would sacrifice animals to show loyalty to the **Pyramidal Empire** and its invisible god.
