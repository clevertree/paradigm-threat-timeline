# Mars (aka Prometheus) replaces Venus as the new Saviour

> *Each new saviour planet carried a dual nature: first as a liberator, then — as it too caused catastrophe — becoming the new archetype of evil. Mars is mythology's most ironic case: the dragon-slayer who was also the dragon.*

## Mars the Saviour: Prometheus, the Fire-Giver

When Venus settled into a less catastrophic orbit after the Exodus, **Priori-Mars** appeared in geosynchronous orbit above the northern hemisphere, looming enormous in the sky. After comet-Venus's terror, a relatively stable, brilliant red body was a relief.

In the [Greek narrative](https://www.ancient.eu/Prometheus/), Prometheus (Mars) thwarted Zeus/Jupiter's plan by forcing humans to invent technology too early. [Pandora's Box](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pandora%27s_box#Difficulties_of_interpretation) was a metaphor for the **Pyramidal Empire** plan to *rule through evil* — strategic release of suppressed technology, usury, religious mind control, greed, disease, war, and death. Electric encounters with Mars quickly taught humans to harness electricity. In the 7th century BCE, humanity enjoyed free energy, ideal health, and no Pyramidal influence as [magnificent cities](https://www.cnn.com/travel/article/worlds-best-ancient-cities/index.html) blossomed worldwide.

Mars-as-saviour across cultures:

- **Greek**: Prometheus — stole fire from the gods for humanity
- **Roman**: Mars Ultor ("Mars the Avenger") — champion against cosmic tyranny
- **Norse**: Tyr — one-handed god of war and justice
- **Hindu**: Karttikeya / Skanda — divine warrior overthrowing demonic forces
- **Mesopotamian**: Nergal — warrior of the underworld, elevated as protector
- **Hebrew**: The Angel Michael — warrior archangel who defeated the Dragon (Venus)

Mars's gift was **fire and electricity** — practical knowledge of plasma, demonstrating on a cosmic scale that the Pyramidal Empire was mortal and defeatable.

## Mars the Destroyer: The Red Dragon

Yet Mars was simultaneously the **most feared object in the ancient sky**. In geosynchronous orbit just 42,000 kilometres above Earth, it periodically exchanged electric discharges causing earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, tsunamis, and global electromagnetic storms.

The ancient world's most vivid monsters are Mars:

- **The Red Dragon** (Revelation 12) — Mars's plasma tail
- **Typhon** (Greek), **Vritra** (Hindu), **Apep** (Egyptian) — the great serpent-monster battling the hero/sun
- **Set** (Egyptian) — red-skinned god of chaos, enemy of Osiris and Horus
- **Ares** (Greek) — despised even by his father Zeus

Homer writes that Zeus says of Ares: *"of all the gods who hold Olympus, I hate you the most."* He was the saviour who terrified his worshippers.

## The Nine Deaths of Mars

With each encounter, [Priori-Mars](https://cycliccatastrophism.org/2010/01/16/the-origin-of-avatar/)'s outer shell collapsed, making the inner shell temporarily visible. The [lightning scars](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tRV1e5_tB6Y) on Mars's surface led humans to believe Prometheus was punished for his gift:

> Eternal chains, bound to a mountain — his liver torn out each day by an eagle, regenerated each night, torn out again.

Nine times they watched Mars's *"flesh"* torn apart and sewn back together. This is both mythological memory of repeated encounters (Mars giving energy, retreating, returning) and a physical description of what was observed: a planet seeming to die and be reborn.

On **February 27, 747 BCE**, Mars bumped Earth's magnetosphere and altered Earth's orbital duration. Calendar keepers introduced a [leap year](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leap_year) every 4 years for the new 6-hour offset. Even this final gift — calendar stabilization — was delivered through catastrophe.

With Mars's final departure, the sky emptied of terrifying close-encounter planets. A new, reliable source of light stepped forward to fill the theological vacuum.
