# Jupiter replaces Saturn as the new Saviour (aka Zeus, Thor, King Arthur)

> *Each new saviour planet carried a dual nature: first as liberator, then — as it too caused catastrophe — becoming the new archetype of evil. Jupiter was no exception.*
>

## Jupiter the Saviour: The Good King

Resolving electric imbalances between planets, Jupiter became [King of the planets](http://www.empirerome.com/wordpress/?page_id=708) and reigned over a 511-year stability period. King Jupiter sat upon the Cosmic Mountain — Mount Olympus, Parnassus, Sinai. This "mountain" was Jupiter's South Pole plasma coma tail. Once per orbit, Jupiter eclipsed the Sun, wearing the Corona as a Crown.

[Pre-Galfridian Arthurian legend](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_Arthur#Pre-Galfridian_traditions) describes King Arthur (Jupiter) orbiting a *round table* with Lancelot (Mars) and lesser knights defending Britain from fire-monsters (Sun), dragons and witches (Venus).

Jupiter-the-Saviour appeared across cultures as **just and benevolent ruler**: **Zeus** (Greek king of gods), **Odin/Thor** (Norse storm-bringer), **Marduk** (Babylonian dragon-slayer), **Indra** (Hindu warrior-king who slew Vritra), **The Dagda** (Celtic "Good God").

## Jupiter the Destroyer: The Jealous Tyrant

The same myths record Jupiter's **jealousy**, **rage**, and eventual **fall**. Zeus devoured wife Metis whole — swallowing wisdom to prevent prophecy. He wielded thunderbolts against his own children.

This is **accurate observational recording**. The plasma discharges from Jupiter's south pole that first brought order were — as Jupiter grew unstable — the same forces causing destruction. At approximately 2349 BCE, Jupiter [disappeared from position](/content/03.the-dark-ages/03.05.00-2349-bce-jupiter-disappears-and-venus-attacks-earth.md) and **consumed Venus** — drawing it into close encounter, causing comet-mode and the Ten Plagues.

Jupiter swallowing Venus: Zeus swallowing Metis (giving "birth" to Athena/Venus from his brow); the Burning Bush at Sinai (Jupiter's plasma discharge); Thor's battle with the Midgard Serpent at Ragnarok.

Jupiter's burning was interpreted as divine signal. As documented in [Jupiter catches fire](/content/03.the-dark-ages/03.06.00-2193-bce-earth-leaves-last-absu-layer-and-jupiter-consumes-venus.md), the coma ignited. The Pyramidal Empire exploited this by introducing "Mo-Zeus" (Moses) — a prophet relaying messages from the burning God. The **jealous, angry God** of the Old Testament — a rebranding of Jupiter's physical violence into theological command.

> "The LORD your God, **who is among you**, is a jealous God…"
> -<cite>[Deuteronomy 6:14-16](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/6-15.htm)</cite>

The word *jealous* is the tell. A purely theological inventor would not describe an invisible God as jealous of visible things. Only a real physical entity — a planet whose worshippers could observe its decline and be seduced by newer, brighter competitors — required the threat: *do not follow other gods*.

## The Fall of the Round Table

The nonlinear orbit grew unstable, resulting in the [Breaking of the Round Table](https://artifactsjournal.missouri.edu/2015/08/king-arthur-and-his-part-in-the-breaking-of-the-round-table/). King Arthur "died" — Jupiter ceased to dominate. The quest for the Holy Grail became humanity's unfulfilled longing.

With Jupiter eclipsed, Venus entered the sky as the new brilliant body — worshipped as **light bringer** and feared as the comet-dragon.
