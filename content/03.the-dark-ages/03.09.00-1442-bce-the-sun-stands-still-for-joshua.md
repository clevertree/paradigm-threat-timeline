# The sun stands still for Joshua

For another 50 years, planet Venus appeared as a Comet in Earth's sky, until it too became 'fully charged' by the Sun's energy, causing a violent flip in polarity. This state transition — functioning similarly to [atomic electron transition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atomic_electron_transition) — forced Venus into a nose-dive towards its final orbit. During this transition, Earth was subject to an incredibly strong *tilt* in the Sun's magnetic current flow, causing the **Sun to appear to reverse its motion in the sky** — as recorded in [Joshua 10-13](http://web.mit.edu/jywang/www/cef/Bible/NIV/NIV_Bible/JOSH+10.html#:~:text=On%20the%20day%20the).

[Velikovsky explains in *Worlds in Collision*](https://books.google.com/books?id=FJst27kSVBgC&pg=PA376):

> The physical effects of retardation or reversal of the earth in its diurnal rotation are differently evaluated by various scientists. Some express the opinion that a total destruction of the earth and volatilization would follow such slowing down. They concede, however, that destruction of such dimensions would not occur if the earth continued to rotate and only its axis were tilted. This could be caused by the earth passing through a strong magnetic field at an angle to the earth's magnetic axis. Theoretically, the terrestrial axis could be tilted for any length of time, and in such a fashion that it would lie in the plane of the ecliptic. In that case, one hemisphere would remain in prolonged day, the other in prolonged night.

In the Biblical Narrative, Moses (Gas-Giant Jupiter) had a successor named Joshua (Planet Venus). With the "Death" of Moses, Joshua leads the Israelites to Palestine. Joshua needs a decisive victory over the [5 kings of Makkedah](https://rpmdaily.net/blog/the-5-kings-of-makkedah-5-in-the-series). To accomplish this, Joshua speaks to an invisible "LORD" and requests intervention. [Joshua 10:13](https://biblehub.com/joshua/10-13.htm):

> The day that the LORD gave the Amorites over to the Israelites, Joshua spoke to the LORD in the presence of Israel: "O sun, stand still over Gibeon, O moon, over the Valley of Aijalon." And the sun stood still, and the moon stayed, until the people had avenged themselves. Is not this written in the book of Jasher? So the sun stood still in the midst of heaven, and hasted not to go down about a whole day.

The Sun's proximity to Venus calms the dragon's rage as Venus transforms back into spherical shape. The orbits of Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, and Jupiter were now stabilising around the Sun. The memory of Venus as Dragon/Comet [fades into myth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_dragons_in_mythology_and_folklore). This natural cataclysm was redacted into an epic of *divine intervention* to serve the **Pyramidal Empire**. To ordinary folk it symbolized the *end* of cataclysm — the Pantheon of Gods taking their leave to be replaced by the one "Lord," our Sun. As planets became invisible, humanity forgot them and [polytheism went into decline](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decline_of_Greco-Roman_polytheism).

[Velikovsky states](https://books.google.com/books?id=FJst27kSVBgC&pg=PA299):

> With Macrobius in the fourth Christian century, there begins a tendency to see in many gods of Egyptian and Greek antiquity the personification of the sun. Macrobius compared Osiris to the sun, and Isis to the moon, disregarding the opinion of earlier authors. He also interpreted Jupiter as the sun. As the role the planets played in the history of the world retreated into oblivion, the interpretation of nature myths as referring to the sun or the moon became more widespread.
