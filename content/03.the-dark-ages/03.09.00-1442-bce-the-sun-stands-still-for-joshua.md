# 1442 BCE — The Sun Stands Still for Joshua

For another 50 years Venus appeared as a comet in Earth's sky until it too became fully charged by the Sun's energy, forcing a violent polarity flip. This [atomic-electron-transition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atomic_electron_transition)-like state change sent Venus into a nose-dive toward its final orbit. During the transition, Earth experienced a powerful tilt in the Sun's magnetic current — causing **the Sun to appear to reverse direction in the sky** — recorded in [Joshua 10–13](http://web.mit.edu/jywang/www/cef/Bible/NIV/NIV_Bible/JOSH+10.html).

![Bayeux Tapestry: The Sun Stands Still for Joshua](/media/03.the-dark-ages/sun_stands_still_joshua_bayeux.jpg)

[Velikovsky explains in *Worlds in Collision*](https://books.google.com/books?id=FJst27kSVBgC&pg=PA376):

> A total destruction would not occur if the earth continued to rotate and only its axis were tilted. This could be caused by the earth passing through a strong magnetic field at an angle to the earth's magnetic axis. Theoretically, the terrestrial axis could be tilted for any length of time, so that it lay in the plane of the ecliptic — one hemisphere in prolonged day, the other in prolonged night.

In the Biblical narrative, Moses (Gas-Giant Jupiter) had a successor named Joshua (Planet Venus). With Moses' "death," Joshua leads the Israelites to Palestine and needs a decisive victory over the [5 kings of Makkedah](https://rpmdaily.net/blog/the-5-kings-of-makkedah-5-in-the-series). [Joshua 10:13](https://biblehub.com/joshua/10-13.htm):

> "O sun, stand still over Gibeon, O moon, over the Valley of Aijalon." And the sun stood still, and the moon stayed, until the people had avenged themselves… So the sun stood still in the midst of heaven, and hasted not to go down about a whole day.

The Sun's proximity calms the dragon's rage as Venus returns to spherical shape. The orbits of the inner planets stabilise. The memory of Venus as Dragon/Comet [fades to myth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_dragons_in_mythology_and_folklore). This natural cataclysm was redacted into an epic of divine intervention serving the **Pyramidal Empire**. To ordinary people it symbolised the end of cataclysm — the Pantheon departing, replaced by the one "Lord," our Sun. As planets became invisible, polytheism went into decline.

[Velikovsky states](https://books.google.com/books?id=FJst27kSVBgC&pg=PA299):

> With Macrobius in the fourth Christian century there begins a tendency to see in many gods of antiquity the personification of the sun. As the role the planets played in the history of the world retreated into oblivion, the interpretation of nature myths as referring to the sun became more widespread.
