This global distribution cannot be explained by Fomenko's theory that they were Horde imperial monuments or Christian Easter-cake symbols — that does not account for pyramids predating Christianity and spanning cultures that had no contact with the Rus.

Pyramids were active during the Dark Ages when the cosmic electrical environment was intense. They are inactive today because the Solar System stabilized and the Birkeland currents between planets ceased.

The construction techniques — geopolymer concrete, megalithic precision — were not invented during the Dark Ages. They were **inherited from the Golden Age**, carried forward by survivors of the cataclysm that ended it. The knowledge was applied out of desperate necessity, not imperial vanity.
