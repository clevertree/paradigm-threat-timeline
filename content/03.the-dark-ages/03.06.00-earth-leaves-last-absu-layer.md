# 2193–2167 BCE — Earth leaves last Absu layer & Seasons Begin

This new period of stability lasted 156 years, ending abruptly when the nonlinear configuration lost its last Absu layer. Exposed to the Sun's electromagnetic field, each planet formed its own [magnetosphere](http://www.plasmacosmology.net/tech.html). Seasons began on Earth for the first time. Desert formation forced populations of Western Asia, the Indian Subcontinent, and North Africa to migrate. The Egyptian and [Akkadian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akkadian_Empire) kingdoms collapsed. Earth remained in nonlinear orbit with Jupiter, and the year extended to 273 days. As Venus became exposed to the Sun's field, it retreated into Jupiter's magnetosphere, once again *consumed into the Gas Giant's interior*.

## Year Extends to 273 Days — Ice Age in the North

Northern populations experienced an ice age lasting over 200 years. Robert Ackerman (*Egyptian Myth and Catastrophism*) explains that Norse mythology preserves Proto-Venus not as heat or drought but as *winter* — because during the Vedic Period, when priori-Mars was released from geosynchronous orbit, the Norse lands would have been much farther from the equator.

## 2167 BCE — Jupiter Catches on Fire

Within 25 years, Jupiter — having recently consumed Venus — became fully exposed to the Sun's field and caught fire for 20 straight years. Humans worldwide remembered this as [The Burning Bush](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burning_bush) (Mosaic), [Chilam Balam](https://www.sacred-texts.com/nam/maya/cbc/cbc15.htm) (Mayan), and many others. The Old Testament redacts the planet saviour Jupiter (Zeus) into a mythical human prophet "Mo-Zeus" representing a '[Universal Creator and Saviour God](https://sites.google.com/a/kent.edu/jwattles/home/comparative-religious-thought/judaism/moses-concept-of-god)'.

Under Jupiter's changing influence, Earth underwent further cataclysm. The ground vibrated, and a trumpeting sound was heard and *felt* by every creature up to [613](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/613_commandments) times. Humans interpreted each exultation as a Commandment spoken by God — the basis of the 10 Commandments of the [Mosaic Covenant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mosaic_covenant). For Jupiter's full mythological role, see [Jupiter replaces Saturn as the new Saviour](/timeline/evt-jupiter-replaces-saturn-as-the-new-saviour-aka-zeu).

Jupiter's epic display meant the powers that be did not represent any gods. The **Pyramidal Empire** reacted by creating the [Abrahamic Covenant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Covenant_of_the_pieces) — replacing direct rulership with indirect religious mind-control. In Genesis 22, the [Binding of Isaac](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Binding_of_Isaac) describes these new rules: Abraham was ordered to sacrifice his son Isaac until stopped by emissaries of the age of Aries, who informed him he could slaughter a ram instead. Going forward, subjects would sacrifice animals to show loyalty to the **Pyramidal Empire** and its invisible god.
