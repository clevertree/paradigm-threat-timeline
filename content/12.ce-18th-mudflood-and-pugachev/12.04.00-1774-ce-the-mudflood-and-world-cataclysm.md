# The MudFlood and World Cataclysm

![MudFlood and Pugachev — the final Absu dispersal](/media/12.ce-18th-mudflood-and-pugachev/mudflood.png)

The *MudFlood Energetic Event* (MFEE) describes a planet-scale calamity that struck Earth in the late 18th century, abruptly and permanently raising ground levels around existing cathedrals, pyramids, and monuments — burying entire civilizations in mud. Photographs, excavation reports, and comparative maps all document cities buried without satisfactory mainstream explanation: no tilting from liquefaction, no debris consistent with hydraulic drilling, no gradual accumulation. The event appears simultaneous and worldwide.

According to the MFEE Hypothesis, the trigger was the [Pugachev Rebellion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yemelyan_Pugachev) of 1773–1774 — the final war between the *Royal* Christian giants descended from Christ and the Romanov-backed forces of smaller humans. The Romanov state, unable to defeat Pugachev's army by conventional means, called upon a breakaway human civilization on Mars to deploy large-scale [Directed Energy Weapons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laser_weapon) against all remaining strongholds loyal to the Rus-Tartarian empire. The secondary discharge backfired, devastating Martian surface cities as well.

The energy event coursed across Earth's surface, tearing up land and hurling matter miles into the air. Positive electric discharge pulled entire mountain ranges from the ground in dendritic patterns; negative discharge carved canyons identical to lightning scarring observed across Mars. This process also explains instantaneous petrification of human giants — who had no shelter large enough to survive — producing what later archaeologists catalogued as *dinosaur fossil graveyards*.

World maps dated before the 19th century record a dramatically different Earth: California appears as an island larger than the United Kingdom, the Grand Canyon and Colorado River are absent, and no Himalayan mountain range is shown. Old maps of Siberia depict a flourishing Tartarian empire with cities, rivers, and natural boundaries — nothing resembling the uninhabitable wasteland it became after the event. To find where the melted Tartarian cities once stood, search a modern map of global gold mine concentrations — the correlation is striking.

The emptied cities were subsequently repopulated via orphan trains, their new inhabitants forming the first cargo cults. The surviving indigenous peoples — the so-called "Red-Skin" Native Americans, direct relatives of the Lamanites — were hunted and relocated across the Bering Strait (the Trail of Tears), after which the Bering Strait itself was destroyed. The Smithsonian Institution, founded in 1846, systematically excavated and suppressed giant skeletal remains across America, reclassifying them as dinosaur fossils and establishing the false narrative that indigenous Americans were Stone Age cave dwellers.

The cover-up was completed through coordinated redaction of all history, religion, science, and language, imposed by those who controlled the repopulation. World religions were *canonized* — the Latin word *canon* meaning both "authority" and a large gun — by the same military force that carried out the event. The ability to erase the past gives those in the present control of the future: all that remained was the managed story, the managed map, and the managed bones.

## The MFEE and the Rise of Communism

The advent of communism can be understood as a direct consequence of the MudFlood Energetic Event. Before the MFEE, Earth hosted an extraordinary variety within the human species: giants descended from the old Tartarian bloodlines, diminutive peoples, and wild-living populations whose bodies were still covered in hair — the hairy "wild men" depicted in medieval heraldry and woodcuts were not myth but memory. This diversity of size, strength, and way of life made centralized ideological control impossible. No single political system could flatten such variety into a uniform, manageable class.

After the MFEE, only normal-sized humans survived in significant numbers. The giants were petrified or killed outright; the small and the wild perished without the ecosystems that sustained them. The survivors had no choice but to cluster into communities and ration whatever stored supplies remained during the prolonged loss of life and loss of light that followed the event. This forced communalism — born of desperation, not ideology — became the template upon which later communist revolutions were built.

The pattern also explains why the French attacked *Russia* rather than England. France and England, despite their theatrical rivalries, were more alike than different — both Latin-alphabet cultures, both post-MFEE homogenized populations, both participants in the same managed narrative. Russia was the real threat: a non-Latin-language civilization that still practiced polytheism, banned usury, and whose people were physically larger, stronger, and more sexually dimorphic — more masculine men, more feminine women. The Rus-Tartarian remnant represented everything the post-MFEE order needed to eliminate. Napoleon's invasion was not a war between rival European powers; it was a cleanup operation against the last visible survivors of the old world.
