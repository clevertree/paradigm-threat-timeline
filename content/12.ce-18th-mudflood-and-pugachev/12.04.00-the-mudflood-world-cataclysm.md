The secondary discharge backfired, devastating Martian surface cities as well.

The energy event tore up land and hurled matter miles into the air. Positive discharge pulled mountain ranges from the ground; negative discharge carved canyons identical to Mars lightning scarring. World religions *canonized* — *canon* = authority and large gun — by the same force. The ability to erase the past gives control of the future.
