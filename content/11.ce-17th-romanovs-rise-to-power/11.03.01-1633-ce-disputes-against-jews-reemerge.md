# 1633 CE — Disputes against Jews reemerge

The breaking up of the Russian Empire created the [Jewish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jewish_diaspora) and [Gypsy / Romani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Romani_diaspora) diasporas. Both groups had traveled freely between vassal states and now found themselves cut off from old imperial ties. Jews found themselves at the highest level of authority in each region — controlling money, trade, and [monarchies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monarchies_in_Europe) — while Gypsies, named after an Empire that no longer existed, became powerless. Disputes between common folk (gentiles) and Jews emerged over every facet of life, from [eating rituals](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kashrut) to [monetary policy](https://mosaicmagazine.com/observation/history-ideas/2019/06/filthy-lucre-a-look-at-the-history-of-jews-and-money/).

## The Imperial Treasury Caste

The New Chronology offers a structural explanation for Jewish survival after the Empire's collapse. In the Hordian Empire, social organization followed a hereditary caste model. The financial officers — managing accounts across Europe, Africa, and America, collecting taxes, controlling precious metal flows, financing the army — formed a distinct professional caste. Their work demanded precision and a strict regulatory mindset (hence the Talmudic legal system's emphasis on financial rules).

This caste operated globally by necessity: an Imperial banker in Egypt and one in England had stronger professional ties than two soldiers in distant provinces. Over generations, this tight-knit guild developed its own religious community, becoming a recognizable strand of what we now call Judaism. Crucially, this guild needed no territorial state — their "country" was the Empire itself. When the Empire collapsed, they retained cosmopolitan mobility and professional networks, while warrior castes and administrative nobles were territorialized and eventually replaced. ([Chronologia, Ch.9 §3](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/09.html))

## Power of Money Erupts

Fomenko identifies the critical transition: in the 16th–17th centuries, as the Empire fractured, the previously subordinate financial caste found itself in unprecedented power. Military governors of new independent states believed they were the victors. They were not. The driving force behind the revolutionary wave sweeping Europe (English Revolution, French Revolution, Dutch Republic) was capital overriding nobility — the [Miser Knight](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Covetous_Knight) pattern: the Imperial knight demanding salary from the treasury official who now controlled the cash.

> *"The driving force behind all such revolutions was money. As a result they openly declared the domination of money over noble rank, over ancestry. This was the motto of the French revolution and of the English revolution. In the Horde Empire there ruled a principle of ancestry and gentility. Nobility was respected and entitled to power. After the revolt of the Reformation, wealth acquired precedence."* — Fomenko, [Ch.9 §3](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/09.html)

The ghettos appearing across Europe after 1615 were not continuation of ancient prejudice but a new phenomenon — response to the Empire's collapse whose treasury system the Jewish community had administered. Once Imperial authority was gone, local kingdoms had no framework for integrating former Imperial officials. A 1615 Lutheran Chronograph records the expulsion of Jews from Worms directly after the Time of Troubles. ([Chronologia, Ch.9 §3](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/09.html))
