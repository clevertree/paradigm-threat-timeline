# Deep State breaks up the Hordian Empire

Placing the Romanov Dynasty at highest authority in Hordian Empire gave the Pyramidal Empire opportunity to rewrite Russian history. The motive: *fracture* the empire into controllable states and maximize clandestine influence over each. This is how the world-wide Hordian Empire was erased into *independent states history* under different names — Russia, Turkey/Atamania as Ottoman Empire, Austria, Germany, Italy, Poland, Switzerland, Denmark, France, Spain, Egypt, England, Middle East, Persia, China, Japan, India, America. Each region had vassal aristocratic family originally married into Hordian Empire. As ties were cut, these families became Kings and Queens of *their own* states.

Although Rus-Horde Empire was broken up, Pyramidal Empire still feared its reemergence and targeted its history and culture. They launched [modernization campaign](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_reform_of_Peter_the_Great) through Romanov puppet [Czar Peter I "The Great"](https://www.history.com/news/peter-the-great-tortured-killed-own-son) to incorporate Russia into *'modern'* Europe. He reformed calendar and [alphabet](https://www.prlib.ru/en/history/619018), moved Russian Headquarters to St Petersburg. Objective: pressure Orthodox Church's autonomy, reducing religious influence. This [Anti-Russian Sentiment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anti-Russian_sentiment#18th_and_19th_centuries) would continue three centuries and is [still active](https://www.rt.com/russia/569147-zakharova-odessa-monuments-history/). Fomenko: Romanovs rewrote Russian history; the Russian-Horde army was declared an evil foreign power that conquered Russia itself.

## Prester John — The Horde's Unanswered Invitation

From the 12th to 17th centuries, Western Europe circulated legends of **Prester John** — a fabulously wealthy Christian king ruling a vast empire somewhere in the East. Scaligerian historians treat this as a quaint medieval myth. Fomenko identifies the reality behind it: Prester John was **Ivan Danilovich Kalita** (Khan Batuy), and his legendary kingdom was the **Russian-Horde Empire** itself ([chronologia.org Ch. 5 §7](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/05.html#the7)):

> *"Mediaeval Western Europe was for some reason strongly convinced in the existence in the East of a vast Kingdom of a certain Christian ruler 'Prester John', THE DESCENDANTS OF WHOM WERE ALLEGEDLY THE GREAT KHANS OF THE 'MONGOL' EMPIRE."*

English chronicles even identified **Genghis Khan as Prester John** — because both names pointed to the same Hordian dynasty. Fomenko further confirms the direct equivalence: **Rus = Kingdom of Prester John** ([chronologia.org Ch. 6 §28](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/06_21.html#the628)).

The most telling episode in the legend is Europe's appeal for military alliance. When the Ottoman armies swept through the Balkans and threatened Christendom's heartland, Western rulers reportedly dispatched envoys east seeking Prester John's aid. They had noticed that these eastern warriors bore the **holy cross** in their armies — despite differing from the West in language, custom, and rite — and reasoned that a shared Christianity might forge an alliance against the Ottomans. According to the legend, Prester John **never replied**.

The Deep State would certainly have had motive to prevent such a union — a joint East-West Christian front would have been unstoppable and would have undermined every strategy of division they depended on. But the answer may be simpler than conspiracy. By the time these appeals reached Moscow, the crumbling Hordian Empire and its soldiers **remembered centuries of Protestant deception**. Western Europe had exploited the [religious schism](/timeline/evt-european-religious-schism-emerges) to fracture the Empire's own provinces, launched the [Inquisition](/timeline/evt-the-holy-inquisition) against Hordian Christians, imposed [Latin](/timeline/evt-latin-is-the-language-of-the-deep-state) as a weapon of cultural erasure, and conspired with the [Romanov usurpers](/timeline/evt-romanovs-dynasty-takes-the-throne) to seize power from within. Why would the Horde ever join them in battle? The silence of Prester John was not mystery — it was memory.

## "To Turn Cattle into People"

Peter I to Danish ambassador (1710): *"To turn cattle into people"* — treating native population as livestock. Fomenko [Ch.7 §3](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/07.html). This framing — treating native Russian-Hordian population as subhuman livestock — reflected Romanov court's self-understanding as Western-aligned foreign occupying power managing conquered population. Peter's reforms (new calendar, reformed alphabet, mandatory European dress, prohibition of women's traditional tower-chambers, mandatory beard-shaving, capital transfer to Petersburg) were designed to sever cultural ties between Russians and Hordian heritage.

Fomenko notes suspicion that Peter who returned from Western journey (1697–1698) was not same person who left — encapsulating radical discontinuity Romanovs introduced. ([Chronologia, Ch.7 §3](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/07.html))

## The Double-Headed Eagle as Crescent-and-Star

Per Fomenko ([Ch.7 §7](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/07.html)), two-headed eagle is transformation of crescent moon with star cross: one head represents Ottoman Turkish branch (crescent), other Russian Hordian branch (cross/star). When Empire fragmented, its symbol split into Orthodox cross, Islamic crescent-and-star, and Jewish six-pointed star. Double-headed eagle survived as residual emblem of states (Russia, Austria, Albania) remembering Imperial inheritance.

## St. Petersburg: An Escape Hatch

Peter's official rationale for relocating capital to St. Petersburg in 1703 — "window to Europe" — does not explain why entire government of largest country needed to move to swamp on Gulf of Finland.

Fomenko's explanation: Moscow was too close to Moscow Tartary. If Siberian-American Horde launched counter-attack (which it nearly did under Pugachev seventy years later), Romanovs would have been trapped. From St. Petersburg, they were days away from ships to Western Europe — to relatives in German courts. Capital move was insurance for occupying foreign dynasty that never felt secure on stolen ground. ([Chronologia, Ch.8 §1](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/08.html))
