On Jan 4th 1642, Charles I entered House of Commons with 400 soldiers attempting to arrest 5 members for treason — they escaped. The regicide caused England to become a Republic, or [Commonwealth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commonwealth_of_England), lasting until 1660 Stuart Restoration. Of [59 Commissioners](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_regicides_of_Charles_I) who signed death warrant, 9 were executed, many died in prison.
