# Cathars are annihilated in a *reversed* "Holy" Crusade & Inquisition

The Cathar annihilation was the first use of the "reversed Crusade" — Europe turning the Horde's own crusading mechanism *against* Hordian populations. This localized reversal became the template for the full-scale **Reverse Crusades** that followed: [1812](/timeline/evt-napoleon-invades-russia) (Reverse Crusade I), 1914 (II), 1941 (III), and the present (IV). The pattern: Western financial powers organize a coalition to destroy Hordian remnants, using the Horde's own military traditions against them.

In the early 17th century, the Pyramidal Empire began brutally eradicating ideological threats to their Reformation. The eventual [annihilation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catharism#Annihilation) of the Cathars signified the end of original *Gnostic* Christianity in Western Europe, replaced by **Catholicism**. The theological mechanism by which indigenous solar religion — in which Christ was an allegory for the Sun's annual zodiacal journey — was overwritten into invisible-God monotheism controlled by Rome is analysed in [Deep State redacts Christianity into Jesuit Catholicism](/timeline/evt-deep-state-redacts-christianity-into-jesuit-cathol). Many Cathars were publicly burned at the stake. This event was remembered in words like *cauterize*, *catharize*, and *catharsis* — all meaning **purging by fire** — yet the word *Catholic / katholikos* still translates to *Holy Katherine* in [ancient greek](https://www.catholic.com/magazine/print-edition/what-does-catholic-mean). The Protestant Reformation, once a movement for victims of the Russian Holy Inquisition, had now executed their own *reversed* Holy Crusade against the remnants of the Russian Empire.

As the Pyramidal Empire vanquished indigenous European religions, it fractured remaining Abrahamic religions into controllable denominations — Christianity in Europe and Russia, Islam in the Ottoman Empire — managed by [Crypto-Judaism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crypto-Judaism). This allowed the Pyramidal Empire to rule by acting as the *will* of the Judeo-Christian God Jehovah a.k.a. [Yahweh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yahweh). Once again, Pyramidal chronologists covered their crimes by pushing records of wars they started into the distant past. Memory of the Russian Hordian Empire faded, redacted into tales of 'ancient' Goths, 'more ancient' Huns, and 'very ancient' Romans.

## Cathars as Volga Scythians

Fomenko's New Chronology provides the most specific identification of who the Cathars were. Per [Ch.7 §12](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/07_08.html):

> *"The Cathars are the Scythians of the Volga river, who came to France in the XIV c. from Russia-Horde during the 'Mongol' conquest. They settled down here and, as the colonizers, created the ruling class."*

The Cathars were the Hordian aristocracy of Southern France — the ruling class governing Languedoc as Empire representatives since the 14th century. Their distinctive theology (the "Pure Ones," rejection of the material world, dual-principle cosmology) was original Gnostic Christianity of the Empire preserved in colonial form.

When the Reformation war reached Southern France, these Cathar-Scythians were defeated militarily in the late 16th and early 17th centuries — completely at odds with conventional Cathar Crusade dating of 1209–1229. The chronological displacement by roughly 400 years was achieved through the Scaligerian system, moving records far enough into the past that survivors could not recognize them as recent history.

The "Cathar castles" of the Languedoc — Peyrepertuse, Montségur, Quéribus and others — are genuine Hordian Gothic fortifications. After the war, they were declared to have "always been Catholic" properties, Hordian construction retroactively reattributed to Catholic feudal lords, and their destruction by Reformation-era gunpowder presented as outcome of a medieval siege. The explosions that destroyed them are still visible in the masonry. ([Chronologia, Ch.7 §12](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/07_08.html))
