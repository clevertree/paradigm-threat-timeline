# The Apocalypse Prophecy of 1666

> **Author's Theory**: This interpretation is my own reconstruction. No online sources correlating these events in this sequence have been found.

## The Year of Our Lord 666

The year 1666 was a **weapon**. Written as <code>ι</code>666, it read as "in the 666th year of the Lord," invoking the [Number of the Beast](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Number_of_the_beast). For a population steeped in apocalyptic Christianity, this date carried existential weight.

> During the first half of the seventeenth century some extravagant notions of the near approach of the Messianic time, and more especially of the redemption of the Jews and their return to Jerusalem, were set forth by Christian writers and entertained by Jews and Christians alike. The so-called apocalyptic year was assigned by Christian authors to the year 1666.
<cite>[JewishEncyclopedia.com](https://www.jewishencyclopedia.com/articles/13480-shabbethai-zebi-b-mordecai)</cite>

I believe London's leadership was **told** of an impending apocalypse for September 1666 — the same technique used in 1492 with [prophetic letters](/timeline/evt-the-apocalypse-crusade-reverse-exodus).

## The Sabbatean Messianic Mania of 1665–1666

London was not alone. Across the Jewish diaspora — Smyrna to Amsterdam, Aleppo to Hamburg — tens of thousands believed 1666 would bring the end of history. **Sabbatai Zevi** was declared agent of redemption by **Nathan of Gaza** in 1665, with the climax expected in 1666. Jewish communities across three continents entered mass mania — abandoning livelihoods, preparing for return to the Holy Land.

Key phrase from the JewishEncyclopedia: **"Jews and Christians alike."** Multi-faith, multi-continent expectation. The question: **who told them all simultaneously?**

### The Dual Collapse: September 1666

Both prophecies collapsed in September 1666. London [burned to the ground](/timeline/evt-sept-6-1666-ce-london-burns-to-the-ground) (13,200 houses, 87 churches, St. Paul's Cathedral). Simultaneously, the Ottoman Sultan arrested Zevi and forced his conversion to Islam. Two religious populations spanning Europe, North Africa, and the Middle East had been convinced — and both shattered in the same month.

Many of Zevi's followers reinterpreted the failure as a necessary mystical step, allowing the Sabbatean sect to persist, eventually spawning the Dönmeh crypto-Jewish community and influencing Frankism.

## The Sequence of Belief and Doubt (London)

The psychological manipulation unfolded in stages:

1. **The Prophecy (pre-1664)**: London's royalty/clergy were told divine judgment would fall in the year 666
2. **The Comet (1664–1665)**: The [Great Comet](/timeline/evt-the-great-comet-of-1664-1665) appeared for 80+ days. Morton called it "sent immediately by God to awake the secure world"
3. **The Plague (1665–1666)**: The [Great Plague](/timeline/evt-the-great-plague-of-1664-1666) killed 100,000 (15% of London)
4. **The Fire (September 1666)**: London burned. Many accused the government, but political will to resist had died in the flames
5. **The Aftermath**: The royal class became convinced they had witnessed genuine divine judgment, setting the tone for three centuries of British imperialism: anything done in God's name was justified

Polish theologian Stanisław Lubieniecki published *Theatrum cometicum* (1668), stating the Great Fire was **punishment from God announced by the 1664 comet**.
