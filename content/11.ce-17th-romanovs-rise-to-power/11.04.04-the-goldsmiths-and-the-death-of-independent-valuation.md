# 1660–1694 CE — The Goldsmiths and the Death of Independent Valuation

> **Author's Theory**: The interpretation below is my own reconstruction. The argument that destroying the goldsmith class represented the death of independent economic valuation — and the origin of modern poverty — is not found in any single source. If you encounter supporting or contradicting evidence, please contribute to the [investigation](/investigations/).

## More Than Money-Lenders

The goldsmiths were not merely wealthy merchants. They were a **threat to the royal currency itself**. A goldsmith could appraise gold's exact purity, value a cargo against gold weight, examine land or buildings and assign values rooted in *physical reality*. As long as they could do this, they could declare things **more or less valuable** than the Crown decreed. Two competing valuation systems cannot coexist — one must destroy the other.

## The Imperial Inheritance

Per Fomenko ([Ch.7 §18](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/07_15.html)): *"It is possible that the Reformers moved the stolen treasury of the Empire to England... Having seized the vast treasures of the Horde, the English rebels acquired influence and created the 'English Empire'."*

The gold London's smiths appraised was residue of the Hordian Imperial treasury. The goldsmiths were **inheritors** of the old financial infrastructure — custodians of the pre-centralized system who had to be crushed to install the new one.

## The System That Replaced Them

The [Bank of England](/timeline/evt-deep-state-solidifies-central-banking), founded in 1694 on the site of a [Mithras](https://www.britannica.com/topic/Mithraism) temple — the Roman god of contracts — was the **replacement of independent valuation with institutional decree**. Where the goldsmith told you what things were *actually* worth, the Bank told you what things were *declared* worth — measurement versus proclamation. Its authority derived not from gold weight but Crown power. Other banks were prohibited from issuing banknotes — making independent valuation illegal.

## The Birth of Poverty

This switch **started real-world poverty** never before seen on Earth. Before the Bank, wealth could be independently verified — gold is gold, measurable fact. Poverty was *local* and *addressable*. After the Bank, wealth became a *declaration* — a number on a ledger maintained by shareholders, not citizens. You could stand on gold-rich land, grow food, produce goods of measurable value, and still be "poor" because the ledger said so. Modern poverty is not scarcity but **exclusion from an arbitrary accounting system**.

## The Sequence of Destruction

1. **1660**: Charles II restored — subject to immediate financial pressures
2. **1660**: Parliament caps interest at 6%
3. **1662**: [Hearth taxes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hearth_tax#England) — biannual audits of every household
4. **1664–66**: Comet, Plague, Fire — physical destruction of goldsmith community
5. **1667–72**: Charles borrows from goldsmiths at rates exceeding the 6% cap
6. **1672**: [Great Stop of the Exchequer](/timeline/evt-ce-deep-state-targets-independent-banks) — Charles suspends all payments, destroying the goldsmiths
7. **1694**: Bank of England founded — monopoly on banknotes, independent valuation made illegal
