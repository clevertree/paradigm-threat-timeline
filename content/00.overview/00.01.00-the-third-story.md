# The Third Story

David Talbott's keynote at **EU2012 Conference: The Human Story** argued all human intellectual history has played out as contest between exactly two stories — and both are incomplete.

![The Third Story](/media/00.overview/the-third-story.png)
*EU2012 Conference lecture. Speaker: David Talbott, founder & director, The Thunderbolts Project.*

---

## The Two Dominant Stories

**Story One — Mythology and Religion:** Oldest human record is saturated with cosmic dramas: gods at war, catastrophic floods, fire from heaven, creation-destruction cycles. Every ancient culture insisted divine powers ruled from sky and eventually departed — and that archaic sky bore no resemblance to today's.

**Story Two — Rational Science:** Predictable modern sky offered no support for myth. Science arose from that scepticism. By 20th century, standard narrative demanded you choose: science vs. myth.

Talbott's claim: *both* are incomplete. Neither understood without a third.

---

## The Key Anomaly

Every ancient culture shared specific mythic archetypes — Golden Age, World Mountain, Dragon, Divine Warrior, Wheel of Heaven, Cosmic Thunderbolt — cross-cultural and mutually reinforcing, yet nothing in current sky explains them. **Cross-cultural agreement at specific detail level cannot be coincidence.** Divergent civilisations independently preserving identical imagery must be recording shared experience.

## Saturn, Venus, and Mars

Following Velikovsky's work on planetary instability, Talbott established:

- **Saturn was original "sun."** Babylonian *Shamash* (sun god) was explicitly Saturn in magical texts. Greek *Helios* and Latin *Sol* referred to Saturn in earliest usage — undisputed by mainstream scholars.
- **Golden Age = age of Saturn as polar sun.** Life "in time of Kronos" describes prior epoch of peace and stable celestial order.
- **Planets were in collinear polar alignment.** Ancient depictions describe planets in vertical axial stack — mythological World Tree, Axis Mundi, cosmic pillar.

**Venus** appeared as terrifying great comet — *mother of comets*. Long flowing hair, feathers, serpentine form, fiery breath: universal comet symbols applied globally to Venus in earliest sources.

**Mars** was warrior god whose weapon — Sanskrit *vajra*, Greek thunderbolt, Odin's bolt, Indra's weapon — was the **cosmic thunderbolt**: bipolar plasma discharge reproducible in laboratory plasma experiments, with embedded cylinders, pinch points, bipolar symmetry.

## Plasma Connection: Rock Art and Anthony Peratt

Anthony Peratt (Los Alamos) matched tens of thousands of rock-art images worldwide to plasma discharge formations reproducible in laboratory. **Stick-man/squatting-man** petroglyph (universal Hawaii to Saudi Arabia) = cross-section of toroidal plasma column. **Chain of arrows** / ladder of heaven = stacked tori along axial discharge filament, confirmed in declassified supercomputer simulations.

Peratt's investigation was entirely independent of Talbott's mythology work. Their convergence at precise morphological detail — beginning when Talbott sent Peratt single rock-art image in 2000 — is presented as falsification test: accidental agreement at this specificity is not possible.

## The Third Story

> *"The first story will never be understood in the absence of the Electric Universe. The second story must be rewritten in the light of the Electric Universe."*

Third story requires: (1) mythology as literal sky-testimony, (2) recently unstable solar system, (3) Electric Universe physics as causal framework, (4) interdisciplinary convergence across myth, plasma physics, rock art, calendar data, and laboratory experiment, (5) open peer review rather than institutional gatekeeping.
