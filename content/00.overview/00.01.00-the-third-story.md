# The Third Story

David Talbott's keynote at **EU2012 Conference: The Human Story** argued all intellectual history has played out as a contest between two stories — and both are incomplete.

![The Third Story](/media/00.overview/the-third-story.png)
*EU2012 Conference lecture. Speaker: David Talbott, founder & director, The Thunderbolts Project.*

## The Two Dominant Stories

**Story One — Mythology and Religion:** The oldest human records are saturated with cosmic dramas: gods at war, catastrophic floods, fire from heaven, creation-destruction cycles. Every ancient culture insisted divine powers ruled from the sky and eventually departed — and that the archaic sky bore no resemblance to today's.

**Story Two — Rational Science:** The predictable modern sky offered no support for myth. Science arose from that scepticism. By the 20th century the standard narrative demanded a choice: science vs. myth.

Talbott's claim: *both* are incomplete; neither can be understood without a third.

## The Key Anomaly

Every ancient culture shared specific mythic archetypes — Golden Age, World Mountain, Dragon, Divine Warrior, Wheel of Heaven, Cosmic Thunderbolt — cross-cultural and mutually reinforcing, yet nothing in the current sky explains them. **Cross-cultural agreement at this specificity cannot be coincidence.** Divergent civilisations independently preserving identical imagery must be recording shared experience.

## Saturn, Venus, and Mars

- **Saturn was the original "sun."** Babylonian *Shamash* was explicitly Saturn in magical texts. Greek *Helios* and Latin *Sol* referred to Saturn in earliest usage — undisputed by mainstream scholars.
- **Golden Age = age of Saturn as polar sun.** Life "in time of Kronos" describes a prior epoch of stable celestial order.
- **Planets were in collinear polar alignment.** Ancient depictions describe planets in a vertical axial stack — the mythological World Tree / Axis Mundi.

**Venus** appeared as a terrifying great comet — *mother of comets*. Long flowing hair, feathers, serpentine form, fiery breath: universal comet symbols applied globally to Venus in earliest sources.

**Mars** was the warrior god whose weapon — Sanskrit *vajra*, Greek thunderbolt, Odin's bolt, Indra's weapon — was the **cosmic thunderbolt**: a bipolar plasma discharge reproducible in laboratory plasma experiments.

## Plasma Connection: Rock Art and Anthony Peratt

Anthony Peratt (Los Alamos) matched tens of thousands of rock-art images worldwide to plasma discharge formations reproducible in the laboratory. The universal **stick-man/squatting-man** petroglyph = cross-section of a toroidal plasma column. The **chain of arrows/ladder of heaven** = stacked tori along an axial discharge filament, confirmed in declassified supercomputer simulations.

Peratt's work was entirely independent of Talbott's mythology research. Convergence at precise morphological detail — beginning when Talbott sent Peratt a single rock-art image in 2000 — is presented as a falsification test: accidental agreement at this specificity is not possible.

## The Third Story

> *"The first story will never be understood in the absence of the Electric Universe. The second story must be rewritten in the light of the Electric Universe."*

The third story requires: (1) mythology as literal sky-testimony, (2) a recently unstable solar system, (3) Electric Universe physics as causal framework, (4) interdisciplinary convergence across myth, plasma physics, rock art, calendar data, and laboratory experiment, (5) open peer review rather than institutional gatekeeping.
