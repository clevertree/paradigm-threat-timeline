# Authors Who Read Ancient Sources as World Cataclysm

These researchers argue that ancient myth, scripture, sacred art, geology, and archaeology are eyewitness records of real planetary events and cosmic disasters within the last 12,000 years. Also includes researchers who challenge mainstream dating AND interpret cataclysm. Chronological work summarized in [Authors Challenging the Mainstream Timeline](/timeline/evt-authors-challenging-chronology).

## The Core Saturnian Network

**Wal Thornhill** — Physicist, co-founder of the Thunderbolts Project. Developed the Electric Universe model providing plasma physics mechanism for the Saturnian polar configuration. [→ Profile](/timeline/evt-ap-thornhill)

**Ralph Juergens** (1924–1979) — Originated the Electric Sun hypothesis: the Sun powered by external galactic current rather than thermonuclear fusion. Foundation for understanding planetary electrical exchanges. [→ Profile](/timeline/evt-ap-juergens)

**David Talbott** — Primary architect of the Saturn Myth reconstruction. Established through comparative mythology that Saturn, Venus, and Mars were arrayed along Earth's polar axis in the Golden Age. [→ Profile](/timeline/evt-ap-talbott)

**Dwardu Cardona** (1937–2018) — Extended Talbott's reconstruction into the pre-configuration "God Star" era when Saturn was a lone protosun. [→ Profile](/timeline/evt-ap-cardona)

**Jno Cook** — Most detailed Saturnian chronology. Synthesized Talbott-Thornhill-Cardona-Cochrane and applied cross-cultural calendar triangulation to produce specific dates. Primary source for this timeline's opening chapters. [→ Profile](/timeline/evt-ap-jno-cook)

## Foundation Texts
