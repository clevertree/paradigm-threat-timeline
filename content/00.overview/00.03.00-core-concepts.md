# Core Concepts

This project rests on eight hard claims. If any one is false, the project fails. We apply the same standard the [SAFIRE Project](/content/00.overview/00.02.03-challenging-established-physics.md) applied: empirical observation, physical replicability, no interpretive exceptions.

## The Eight Falsifiable Propositions

### 1. Saturn was Earth's original polar companion

Before 4,077 BC, Saturn occupied a position directly above Earth's north pole in collinear electromagnetic alignment including Venus and Mars — the Golden Age, World Tree, Axis Mundi, Polar Sun. **If false:** Every mythological correlation collapses.

### 2. Earth's orbital year length changed in discrete steps

Orbital period changed in quantized jumps — 225, 240, 260, 273, 360, 365.24 days — each triggered by electromagnetic close-encounters. Ancient cultures reformed calendars after each jump. **If false:** Sumerian king-list numbers are meaningless, Tzolkin is arbitrary, convergence of independent calendar reforms is coincidence.

### 3. The Solar System operates on electromagnetic, not purely gravitational, physics

Planets are charged bodies in Sun's plasma environment. Close approaches produce plasma discharges and orbital perturbations — same physics SAFIRE Project demonstrated at bell-jar scale. **If false:** No mechanism for orbital jumps, fossilisation events, or catastrophe record.

### 4. Ancient mythology is literal astronomical testimony

Gods, monsters, sky-battles, and creation events in every culture's texts are eyewitness accounts of real plasma events, not metaphors or psychology. **If false:** Primary source base is worthless as historical data.

### 5. Multiple catastrophic global events occurred within human record

World Flood (~3,147 BC), Venus encounters (~2,349 BC, ~2,193 BC, ~1,492 BC), Mars encounters (~776–686 BC), and 1774 CE petrification event are real physical events within human memory. **If false:** Catastrophes defining this project's chapter structure never happened on required timescale.

### 6. Conventional dating methods require recalibration

Radiocarbon, dendrochronology, and stratigraphy assume stable orbital environment. If Earth's orbit was shorter in antiquity — closer to Sun, higher solar flux — then C-14 production rates, tree-ring widths, and varve counts were all different. **If false:** Conventional dates stand uncorrected.

### 7. The conventional timeline has been artificially extended

[Illig's Phantom Time](/content/00.overview/00.03.02-timeline-synchronization.md) (~297 years fabricated) is correct in substance. Broader Fomenko claim — Scaligerian chronology extends antiquity by centuries through phantom duplications — is also correct. See [Other Challenges to Chronology](/content/04.the-blip/04.02.00-other-challenges-to-chronology.md). **If false:** Timeline synchronization is invalid.

### 8. Fossilisation is an electrical, rapid process

Evidence from Alberta power-line incident (E.R. Milton), Peter Mungo Jupp's research, and Thornhill's fulgurite demonstrations shows fossilisation occurs instantaneously under sufficient electrical discharge. SAFIRE Project confirmed plasma can transmute elements. **If false:** Deep time is real and fossil record is millions of years old.

## How They Interlock

These are not independent — they form single structure:

- **Proposition 3** (plasma physics) provides mechanism for 1, 2, and 5
- **Proposition 1** (Saturn configuration) is origin point of 5's catastrophe sequence
- **Proposition 2** (variable year) provides chronological key making 7 (phantom time) solvable
- **Proposition 4** (mythology as testimony) provides source base for 5's dating
- **Proposition 6** (dating recalibration) is methodological consequence of 2
- **Proposition 8** (rapid fossilisation) is geological consequence of 3 and 5

Disprove mechanism (Proposition 3) and entire structure falls.

## What We Do Not Claim

This project does not require specific theology, agreement on which mythological source is most accurate, literal correctness of any particular Fomenko chronology, or Young-Earth Creationism. It requires only that eight propositions survive empirical scrutiny.
