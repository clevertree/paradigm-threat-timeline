# Cosmic Life Cycle

We are taught our Universe began with a [Big Bang](https://www.nytimes.com/2023/09/02/opinion/cosmos/cosmology-crisis-webb-telescope.html), yet every prediction of this theory has been [refuted](https://www.lppfusion.com/science/cosmic-connection/plasma-cosmos/cosmos/cosmology/the-growing-case-against-the-big-bang/) by data since the 1990s. Every civilization remembering the Golden Age described it starting with a "big bang" or incredible brightness. A *Big Bang* event indicates the moment of *rebirth* in each Solar System's life cycle, not the entire Universe.

![Cosmic Life Cycle of a Solar System](/media/00.overview/cosmic-lifecycle.png)

First, our Sun [converts](https://energyeducation.ca/encyclopedia/Nuclear_fusion_in_the_Sun) light gasses to heavy elements which fall to the Sun's interior and combine into a [proto-planet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Protoplanet) with lightning scars and no life, orbiting from the *inside*. As it grows, energy charges the proto-planet like a capacitor, forcing it to develop hollow spheres to contain energy, generating stronger [magnetic field](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/physics-and-astronomy/planetary-magnetic-field).

After becoming too big for stable orbit within the Sun, the proto-planet is [violently born](https://www.bitchute.com/video/qDnih8aLae1w) from the Sun's surface (a [coronal mass ejection](https://www.nbcnews.com/id/wbna46725245)). Its positively-charged outer EM field pushes it past the [outer solar system](https://science.nasa.gov/planetary-science/focus-areas/outer-solar-system/) toward nearest negative surface charge — like Brown Dwarf Saturn. Once impregnated, the Dwarf's charge changes and it falls toward nearest positive body — our Sun.

![Before the Golden Age, Proto-Saturn was a Brown Dwarf System floating through our galaxy.](/media/00.overview/brown_dwarf.jpg)

To become a planet, a proto-planet must be consumed by a larger host and carried back to Sun. Proto-Earth was consumed by Proto-Saturn and carried into Sun's orbit — our planet's *conception*. See [Before Creation](/timeline/evt-before-creation).

Unable to contain charging bodies within, plasma sheath exploded, creating gas planets and pouring material into Sun's orbit. This material coalesced around [Birkeland currents](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Birkeland_current) forming moons and rock planets, forcing all planets into [collinear configuration](http://qdl.scs-inc.us/2ndParty/Pages/11007.html) connected at their poles. This high-energy period is the Golden Age — when all forms of life are created.

Planets grow; collinear system breaks into non-linear 'round-table' system marked by [epic cataclysms](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/632271) spanning Dark Ages (Old Testament). After instability resolves, planets violently break away to find final orbits. Modern history begins (New Testament).

After thousands of stable years, each planet loses density, drifts outward, eventually [rips](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_Rip) out of solar system. Sun's system empties, ready for another cycle.

## Theoretical Context

This model — **short cosmic life cycle** at Solar System scale — is original synthesis from Saturnian Cosmology, Electric Universe theory, and ancient testimony. [Wal Thornhill](https://www.thunderbolts.info/wp/2012/03/20/the-electric-universe/) and [EU framework](https://www.holoscience.com/wp/electric-universe-predictions/) are consistent, but Thornhill has not explicitly stated Solar Systems undergo birth-death-rebirth cycle.

### Gap Theory (Ruin-Restitution)

Most aligned existing theory holds indefinite interval exists between [Genesis 1:1](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Genesis+1&version=KJV) and [Genesis 1:2](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Genesis+1&version=KJV) — Hebrew *tohu wa-bohu* ("formless and empty") implies *ruined* state, not original one. Cosmos existed, was destroyed, was *recreated*. This aligns: **universe was already there before Genesis**. Proponents: [Thomas Chalmers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_Chalmers) (1814), [G.H. Pember](https://archive.org/details/earthsearliestag00pembuoft) (1876), [Scofield Reference Bible](https://archive.org/details/holybiblescofield00scof) (1909).

### Young Universe Creationism (YUC)

Interprets Genesis literally, placing cosmos at 6,000–10,000 years. While cosmic life cycle model agrees *current cycle* may be young — consistent with Fomenko and Saturnian timelines — it does not require *universe itself* to be young: prior cycles may have preceded our own.
