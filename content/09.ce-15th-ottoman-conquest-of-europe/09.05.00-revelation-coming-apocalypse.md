# Revelation coming Apocalypse

With new propaganda tools and weapons, the **Deep State** had means to cause war in Europe. A *secret* plan would have been exposed and failed. Instead, they placed the plan **in plain sight** for everyone who could read Latin. The book of this *planned apocalypse* is [The Book of Revelation.](https://www.cliffsnotes.com/literature/n/new-testament-of-the-bible/summary-and-analysis/the-book-of-revelation)

## The Letters to the Seven Churches

Fomenko demonstrates Revelation was written in **1486 CE** — six years before the planned apocalypse of 1492 — describing not a mystical future but a **concrete military operation** ([chronologia.org Ch. 5 §38](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/05_38.html)). In 1486, the Deep State sent letters to [7 major churches](https://www.cliffsnotes.com/literature/n/new-testament-of-the-bible/summary-and-analysis/the-book-of-revelation) in Asia Minor purporting divine revelation — specifically impending military incursion of Western Europe using [armed cavalry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Four_Horsemen_of_the_Apocalypse) to curtail disease by **destroying the diseased**. Priests justified slaughter by portraying victims as sinners incurring God's wrath. For hundreds of thousands of *marked sinners*, this was the **End of Days**. Those able fled to America on [large flotillas](https://www.history.com/news/christopher-columbus-ships-caravels).

These were not pastoral epistles but **operational communiqués** — announcing the campaign, identifying loyalties, and flushing out opposition.

## Revelation of the Method

This is the intelligence technique **[Revelation of the Method](https://www.counterpunch.org/2022/04/29/revelation-of-the-method/)** (RoM): **announce the plan openly before executing it.**

> *"Realizing that our activities will sooner or later come to light, we structure our activities so that as conspiracy researchers unravel them, they will release information into the public consciousness in such a way that it mirrors our initiatory procedure... Success can then be measured precisely to the extent that our work is exposed."*
> — Quoted in Michael A. Hoffman II, *Secret Societies and Psychological Warfare* (2001)

Hoffman also writes:

> *"The purpose behind the 'Revelation of the Method' is the implied consent of the populace. If no resistance is raised when they reveal the truth, the cryptocracy is free to continue."*

### The Revelation Letters as RoM

The letters to the seven churches are **the earliest documented instance of Revelation of the Method** — a war plan announced in advance, dressed in divine authority:

1. **Identify friends and foes.** Those who submit are allies; those who resist reveal themselves as targets.
2. **Demoralize opposition.** When the enemy announces their plan and you cannot stop it, paralysis results. Many fled rather than fought.
3. **Establish perceived inevitability.** Framing the plan as divine prophecy makes resistance *blasphemous*.
4. **Create plausible deniability.** Once complete, the communiqués are reframed as "mystical prophecy" — which is exactly what happened when canonized into the [Book of Revelation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Book_of_Revelation) in the 18th century.

The strategy was highly effective. Those wishing to remain in Europe and avoid slaughter had to abandon ancient traditions and submit to the Greek Orthodox Church of the Rus-Horde Empire.
