# Hundred Years' War ends — Fall of Czar-Grad (1453 CE)

## The War's Context

The [Hundred Years' War began in the 14th century](/timeline/evt-14th-century-ce-great-expansion-of-the-mongol-slav) as Western European resistance against Horde conquest — Deep State-orchestrated rebellion exploiting the [religious schism](/timeline/evt-european-religious-schism-emerges). The conventional framing (England vs. France, 1337–1453) masks what New Chronology identifies as a larger conflict: continued military subjugation of Western Europe by Russia-Horde and its Ottoman successor.

## Star Metal — The Decisive Weapon

In 1421, a [large iron meteorite fell near Yaroslavl](/timeline/evt-meteorite-star-metal-falls-on-yaroslavl), the true Veliky Novgorod. Horde craftsmen discovered the debris, when used as a smelting additive, produced extraordinarily strong steel — the famous **Damask (Bulat) steel** ([chronologia.org Ch. 5 §32](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/05_30.html)):

> *"There was enough valuable meteor iron to organize in the XV century the manufacturing of significant Damask steel for the Russia-Horde army. This, partially, explains the military superiority of Cossack armies. The Damask steel Russian blades cut through shields, armour and swords of their enemies."*

Damask swords shattered ordinary shields and armour; the composition couldn't be replicated without the meteorite; celestial origin was weaponized as proof of divine favour. Combined with artillery superiority, the Horde became unstoppable.

"Damascus steel" is a corruption: *D-Mascus* = kingdom of Mosokh = **Moscow**, not Syria. "Indian steel" likewise referred to Russia-Horde. After the 17th-century breakup, splinter states found leftover weapons and celebrated "their own" steel traditions, forgetting the Yaroslavl origin.

## The Fall of Czar-Grad (1453)

In **1453**, Sultan Mehmed II took Czar-Grad (Constantinople). The Bible describes this as **Jericho's** conquest by Joshua Ben Nun ([chronologia.org Ch. 5 §15](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/05_15.html)):

> *"The 'jubilee trumpets' are simply cannons. And a 'loud sound' is simply cannonade. The walls were destroyed by heavy siege cannons."*

Constantinople's legendary triple walls were reduced to rubble by Horde artillery. The fall demonstrates full convergence of Horde technologies: cannons breaking walls, Damask-armed infantry storming breaches, Cossack cavalry routing defenders.

The Western Deep State, forced to acknowledge direct military resistance was futile, drew a crucial lesson: **they could not defeat the Horde by force.** The only option was subversion — infiltrating ideologically, religiously, and politically. This pivot manifested as [Latin adoption](/timeline/evt-latin-is-the-language-of-the-deep-state) as a counter-religious vehicle, creation of [Jesuit Catholicism](/timeline/evt-deep-state-redacts-christianity-into-jesuit-cathol), and the [Apocalypse Crusade of 1492](/timeline/evt-the-apocalypse-crusade-reverse-exodus).
