# Jesuit Operations: Conversion, Catholicism, and the New World

![Jesuit Operations](/media/09.ce-15th-ottoman-conquest-of-europe/apocalypse_vasnetsov.jpg)

The Jesuits — the [Secret Society of Jesus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monita_Secreta) — were the primary instrument for spreading reformed monotheism globally. Founded in 1534 by Ignatius of Loyola, the Society operated along quasi-military lines: learn the language, document the culture, build trust, identify the local deity (almost universally the Sun), and redirect worship toward Rome-controlled Catholicism.
