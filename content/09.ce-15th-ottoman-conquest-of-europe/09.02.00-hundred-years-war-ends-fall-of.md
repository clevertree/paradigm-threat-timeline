# Fall of Czar-Grad (Constantinople)

In **1453 CE**, Sultan Mehmed II conquered Czar-Grad — known conventionally as Constantinople. This marked the decisive conclusion of the [Hundred Years' War](/timeline/evt-14th-century-ce-great-expansion-of-the-mongol-slav), which conventional history frames as "England vs. France" but which New Chronology identifies as Western Europe's failed resistance against the Russia-Horde and its Ottoman successor.

## The Biblical Jericho

Fomenko identifies the Fall of Constantinople as the conquest of **Jericho** by Joshua Ben Nun ([chronologia.org Ch. 5 §15](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/05_15.html)):

> *"The 'jubilee trumpets' are simply cannons. And a 'loud sound' is simply cannonade. The walls were destroyed by heavy siege cannons."*

Constantinople's legendary triple walls — considered impregnable for a thousand years — were reduced to rubble by Horde artillery. [Star Metal from the Yaroslavl meteorite](/timeline/evt-meteorite-star-metal-falls-on-yaroslavl) armed the infantry that stormed the breaches. Cossack cavalry routed the defenders. Every military advantage the Horde had built over the preceding decades converged in this single siege.

## The Deep State Pivots to Subversion

The fall of Czar-Grad forced a strategic reckoning. The Western [Deep State](/timeline/evt-the-deep-state-the-census-and-the-naming-of-christ), having failed to resist the Horde militarily through [Joan of Arc](/timeline/evt-jeanne-darc-is-executed-in-rouen-france) and other provincial rebellions, drew a crucial lesson: **they could not defeat the Empire by force.**

The only remaining option was subversion — infiltrating the Empire ideologically, religiously, and politically from within. This pivot produced the defining strategies of the next century:

- **[Latin](/timeline/evt-latin-is-the-language-of-the-deep-state)** adopted as a counter-religious vehicle, a secret language of the conspirators
- **[Jesuit Catholicism](/timeline/evt-deep-state-redacts-christianity-into-jesuit-cathol)** engineered as a theological weapon to fracture Imperial Christianity
- **[The Apocalypse Crusade of 1492](/timeline/evt-the-apocalypse-crusade-reverse-exodus)** — the culmination of these strategies

The year 1453 is the hinge point of the 15th century: before it, the Deep State fought openly and lost. After it, they fought covertly — and eventually won.
