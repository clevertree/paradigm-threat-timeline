# The Giants of the Rus-Horde

![The Giants of the Rus-Horde](/media/07.ce-13th-the-russian-horde-tartarian-empire/giants-of-the-rus-horde.png)

The word **Horde** (Russian: *Orda*) derives from *RAT'* (рать), meaning **army**; *RUTHENIA* meant *RATNAYA* — a **military** country. "Mongol" comes from *MNOGO* (много, "many") or Greek *MEGALION* ("great") ([Fomenko, ch.3 §7](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/03_7.html)). The mistranslation of *Orda* as "horde" — conjuring disorderly savages — obscures a disciplined imperial civilisation whose leadership caste were **giant, hairy humans**.

## The Giant Leadership

The Rus-Horde's ruling class — descendants of the [Wild People](/timeline/evt-hairy-mary) — were of enormous stature, covered in body hair, and venerated as living connections to the pre-catastrophe world. Christ himself, born of the hairy giant [Mary of Rus'](/timeline/evt-hairy-mary), founded this dynasty. They possessed knowledge systems, religious traditions, and physical capabilities that made them ungovernable by the Deep State.

## The Codex Gigas: A Giant's Bible

The [Codex Gigas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Codex_Gigas) — "Giant Book" — is the largest extant medieval manuscript: **92 cm tall, 50 cm wide, 74.8 kg**, 310 vellum leaves from ~160 donkey skins. A 36-inch, 165-pound book cannot be used at a normal desk. It was made for a **giant reader**. The "Devil" depicted inside — green-skinned, hairy, horned — is a demonised portrait of the race the book served. The first page includes **Early Cyrillic and Glagolitic alphabets** — scripts of Rus'.

## Three Wars Against the Giants

1. **The Crusade (early 12th century)** — first campaign against giant populations, triggered by Christ's [Crucifixion](/timeline/evt-historical-christ-dies) and a Deep State census targeting giants near Istanbul.
2. **[Battle of Kulikovo (1380)](/timeline/evt-giants-are-defeated-at-the-battle-of-kulikovo)** — cannon technology defeated the giants. "Royal" Christianity fell to Apostolic Christianity.
3. **[Pugachev Rebellion (1773–1775)](/timeline/evt-the-rebellion-of-pugachev)** — the final war. Pugachev led giants and humans against the Romanovs. Defeat by [directed energy weapons](/timeline/evt-the-mudflood-and-world-cataclysm) ended giant civilisation. Remaining giants were [instantly fossilised](/timeline/evt-instant-fossilization-and-electric-petrification) and their bones reclassified as dinosaur fossils.
