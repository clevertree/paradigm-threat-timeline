Andronicus Christ — first emperor of Rus Horde Empire — spoke against usury and slavery, but Horde still imposed taxation and conscription on conquered vassals. The army was predominantly horse cavalry (horses native to Volga-Don steppes), enabling rapid movement over great distances. To feed army and vassals, *slash and burn* farming was invented: forests incinerated to create agricultural land. Deforestation continued ~200 years until ~1503, when Horde switched to three-field rotation after recognizing soil dries up after 30 years without reclamation.

## The Second Golden Age

The 13th century was **second Golden Age** — brief, explosive, unprecedented since fall of [Atlantean civilization](/timeline/evt-atlantis-and-the-tree-of-knowledge).

When Crusaders breached Troy and captured Jerusalem #1 (Istanbul), they cracked open Deep State's vault. Temple of Jerusalem contained accumulated secrets of old order: Atlantean technology, suppressed sciences, astronomical records, engineering principles. The [Masons](/timeline/evt-the-masons) who built empire's great structures were first beneficiaries — speed of construction across three continents testifies to quality of what they found.

Rus-Horde did not hoard this knowledge — they *taught* it. Empire was **first human republic on Earth**, built on singular form of authority: **sacrificial legitimacy**. Christ — giant prince of Rus' — had been crucified by Deep State, and his martyrdom was foundational event. No other family could claim their ancestor died for the people and *still* risen to conquer world in his name. People followed willingly. Atlantean secrets shared openly. For three generations, humanity experienced what it had not known since before Dark Ages: rulers who served the ruled.

## The Taint Within the Bloodline

Deep State had not been destroyed at Jerusalem — it *relocated*. Target was **bloodline itself**: single point of failure. As long as genuine descendant of Christ sat on throne, usury, manufactured religion, and artificial scarcity had no purchase. Republic was immune because built on something they could not counterfeit.

So they married into bloodline, placed advisors near heirs, introduced dependencies weakening each successive generation. Within three generations, taint had taken hold. Rulers who bore Christ's name no longer carried his will. Knowledge began to be restricted — not by Horde, but by parasitic class now embedded within it.

This was [Oprichnina](/timeline/evt-romanov-dynasty-stages-the-oprichnina-coup) in embryo — mechanism by which Deep State would seize throne outright, installing [Romanov dynasty](/timeline/evt-17th-century-from-empire-to-invisible-government) centuries later. Pattern identical to every Deep State infiltration: they cannot build, create, or lead — but can corrupt what others build, and they are infinitely patient.
