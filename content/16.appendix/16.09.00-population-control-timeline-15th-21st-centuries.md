# Population Control: A Timeline (15th–21st Centuries)

This appendix expands on [Population Control: From 1492 to the Technocratic Grid](/timeline/evt-population-control). It documents the recurring pattern — since the first major man-made apocalypse of 1492 — of periodic population reduction to restore Deep State order or engineer cultural revolution.

---

## 15th Century: The First Man-Made Apocalypse

**[The Apocalypse Crusade](/timeline/evt-the-apocalypse-crusade-reverse-exodus) (1492)** — The Rus/Ottoman invasion of Europe represents the first major documented population-control operation. Fomenko identifies it as a deliberate campaign: disease (venereal plagues from bacchanalian cults) spread along trade routes; the Khans chose to "wipe out the diseased population using military force." The Crusade swept through Western Europe, converting or killing populations, transforming the Hordian Empire into a slave empire under Deep State influence. Hordian troops attacked from Siberia into Alaska and south through the Americas — remembered as Aztec genocide of the Mayans. The pattern: disease magnifies fear, panic produces reordering of authority, populations are culled or subordinated. This template would repeat for five centuries.

## 17th Century: Predicted Apocalypse and Displacement

**[London Burns to the Ground](/timeline/evt-sept-6-1666-ce-london-burns-to-the-ground) (Sept 2–6, 1666)** — The Great Fire of London was a predicted apocalypse. It coincided with the end of the Great Plague (1664–1666), the Cestui Que Vie Act of 1666, and the replacement of medieval London with a regularized street grid. While direct loss of life was limited, the event produced **mass displacement** and reorganization of the city into a Deep State financial hub. The Cestui Que Vie Act created the legal presumption that persons absent from estate for seven years could be declared legally dead — with estates administered by others. Vatican-influenced canon law deemed native sovereigns "dead at sea," removing sovereignty via birth certificates and creating the secret cestui que vie trust. London became the staging ground for a pattern the Deep State would repeat worldwide: displace populations through catastrophe or war, then convert sovereign natives into property of the state. The cestui que vie trust — converting sovereign natives into property of the state at birth via birth certificate — extends this mechanism to the present.

## 19th–20th Century: Institutional Suppression

**[The Flexner Report](/timeline/evt-flexner-bioelectric-suppression) (1910)** — Rockefeller and Carnegie funding restructured American medical education to exclude electrotherapy, vitalism, homeopathy, and any model of "energy in the body." Schools teaching bioelectric medicine were defunded; physicians using electronic devices were criminalized. The result: generations denied health-extending science. Population control through **suppressed medicine** — life and healing withheld by institutional gatekeeping.

**[Orphan Trains, Trail of Tears, Siberian Exile](/timeline/evt-orphan-trains-trail-of-tears-siberian-exile)** — Mass displacement and family destruction as population-control mechanisms. Indigenous removal, children shipped to frontier families, political dissidents exiled — all convert populations into controllable assets.

## 21st Century: Soft and Hard Attrition

**Direct poisoning** — Food adulteration, pollution, questionable vaccines, harmful radiation (medical over-imaging, EMF saturation). No declared war; attrition through contaminated supply chains.

**Engineered scarcity** — Food shortage, starvation, bee population collapse, ecosystem degradation. Solar-dimming proposals (blocking the sun to prevent crops). Herbicide spraying that kills or contaminates harvests. The Gaza conflict exemplifies **attrition through denial** — food, water, and medicine withheld until populations break.

**Reproductive surrender** — Encouragement to forgo families, voluntary sterilization, framing children as burdens. Dependency on state and technology replaces family bonds.

**Body modification and implants** — Piercings, tattoos, surgical augmentation, chip and hardware insertion. Populations bound to master technology; lower classes as in *War of the Worlds* — essentially slaves dependent on those who control the machines. The end goal: resistance impossible and unthinkable.

## Georgia Guidestones (1980–2022)

The Georgia Guidestones inscribed in eight languages: "Maintain humanity under 500,000,000 in perpetual balance with nature." The stones were destroyed in 2022 — but the directive they announced has been operational for centuries. Population control is not future speculation; it is documented policy, executed through war, plague, displacement, suppressed medicine, and engineered scarcity.
