## Jno Cook: Creation as Process

Different civilizations set year-zero at different chapters of the same ~8,000-year sequence:

| Event | Date | Who counted from this |
|-------|------|----------------------|
| Saturn drops coma, goes nova — "sun first shows itself" | **4077 B.C.** | Quiche Maya |
| World Flood: polar configuration collapses | **3147 B.C.** | Maya Long Count, Sumerian tradition |
| Fall of the Absu: "flood of Noah" | **2349 B.C.** | Biblical/Babylonian tradition |
| End of Mars-Earth conflicts; skies go quiet | **685 B.C.** | "Start of history, science, and philosophy" |

> *"In 4077 BC Saturn dropped its coma... Saturn went nova and lit up more brilliantly than the Sun. To humans who had not clearly seen the real Sun for thousands of years, this was the start of creation, the start of time."* — Jno Cook
