## H.G. Wells (1866–1946): The Template

Wells set the pattern. **Crystal Egg** (1897): crystal sphere provides real-time visual surveillance of Mars — observer on Earth watches Martians; Martians watch back. *"Sent hither from that planet, in order to give the Martians a near view of our affairs."* Two-way scrying device identical to Tolkien's Palantíri and Stargate remote-viewing tech (declassified 1995). Same technology: guard a realm → use for evil.

*War of the Worlds* (1898) inverted prior Mars fiction (benign Martians) into invasion narrative. **Heat ray** — invisible, capacitor-driven — matches U.S. [Active Denial System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Active_Denial_System). 2017 sequel (*The Massacre of Mankind*) restored invisible beam one year after Havana syndrome. **Red weed** six years before [American chestnut blight](/timeline/evt-american-chestnut-blight). *War in the Air* (1908): aerial bombing a decade early. *The World Set Free* (1914): coined "atomic bomb" thirty years before Hiroshima. Fabian insider; [Florence Deeks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Florence_Deeks) demonstrated plagiarism of *The Outline of History*.

### The Plattner Story (1896) and Davidson's Eyes (1895)

*Plattner Story*: chemistry teacher into **fourth spatial dimension** via explosion; returns **mirror-reversed** (heart on right) — dimensional displacement. *Davidson's Eyes*: spontaneous remote viewing — man in London sees in real-time through lighthouse keeper on South Sea island. Both normalise interdimensional/remote-viewing as fantasy a century before U.S. government admission.

## Arthur Machen (1863–1947)

*Great God Pan* (1894): brain surgery opens dimensional perception; patient sees beyond veil, goes insane. Lovecraft's *From Beyond*: same mechanism, electronic. *Angels of Mons* (1915): fiction published as news; public believed it true — documented fiction→reality; fact→fiction works the same way.

## Algernon Blackwood (1869–1951)

*The Willows* (1907): entities detect humans through thought; geographic veils. *John Silence* (1908): occult intelligence operative — physician detecting non-human influence. Encodes: such knowledge is real, learnable, operational — filed as fiction.
