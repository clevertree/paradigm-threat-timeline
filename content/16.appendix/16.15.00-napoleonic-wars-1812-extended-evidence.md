## Russian Mountains (*Montagnes Russes*)

The French encountered elaborate ice slides — massive wooden-framed ramps (20–25m) coated in ice, sleds at tremendous speed. St. Petersburg had permanent installations. The French had nothing comparable. After the occupation, French engineers built wheeled versions in Paris — the ancestor of the modern [roller coaster](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roller_coaster). Roller coasters are still called *"Russian Mountains"* in French, Spanish, Italian. Given Hordian engineering capabilities, these installations likely existed far longer in the east.

## Napoleon's Height and Western Technology

Contemporary caricatures uniformly depict Napoleon as diminutive (~4'). Western populations bred smaller; height differential with Russian population was real. Western armies compensated with technology generations ahead — electromagnetic or plasma-based systems inherited from the breakaway civilization that had [fired on Earth from Mars](/timeline/evt-the-mars-catastrophe) during Pugachev. Floating stone airships, directed-energy siege weapons — devastating in temperate conditions, never tested against Russian winter.
