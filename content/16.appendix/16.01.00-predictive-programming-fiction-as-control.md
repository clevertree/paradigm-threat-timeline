# Predictive Programming: Fiction as Control

![Predictive Programming](/media/15.ce-21st-the-final-struggle/predictive-programming.png)

**Predictive programming** embeds historical truths or planned events into entertainment so audiences fail to recognise reality or dismiss it as coincidence. It operates in two directions: **forward programming** (announcing planned events) and **backward programming** (reframing actual events as myth). This article focuses on backward programming.

## The Wizard of Oz: Atlantis as Children's Entertainment

The 1900 novel and [1939 film](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Wizard_of_Oz_(1939_film)) encode the structure of [Atlantis](/timeline/evt-atlantis-and-the-tree-of-knowledge): **Emerald City** = Poseida (crystal capital); **Yellow Brick Road** = the Bifröst plasma bridge; **Wizard behind the curtain** = the priest class hoarding knowledge. "Pay no attention to the man behind the curtain" presents authority as theatrical.

## Giants: Memory Converted to Fairy Tale

Eliminating giant populations required eliminating memory of them — converting encounters into **children's stories** framing giants as monsters: *Jack the Giant Killer* (1711), *Jack and the Beanstalk* (1807 — 32 years after Pugachev's defeat), *The BFG*, *Attack on Titan*, Harry Potter's Frost Giants. Consistency across centuries suggests coordination. See [Giants of the Rus-Horde](/timeline/evt-the-giants-of-the-rus-horde).

## War of the Worlds: Real Event as Fiction

H.G. Wells [demonstrably plagiarized](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Florence_Deeks) Florence Deeks. His **heat ray** — invisible, capacitor-driven — matches the U.S. Military's [Active Denial System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Active_Denial_System); adaptations turned it into a visible laser; the 2017 sequel restored the Wellsian version one year after Havana syndrome. Before Wells, Mars fiction portrayed Martians as benign; Wells deliberately inverted this. [Orson Welles' 1938 broadcast](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_War_of_the_Worlds_(1938_radio_drama)) used "Revelation of the Method" — declaring "this is fiction" while broadcasting realistic reports. Wells's **red weed** appeared six years before the [American chestnut blight](/timeline/evt-american-chestnut-blight) (1904), killing four billion trees.

## Death on Mars: The Research Trap

Brandenburg's *Death on Mars* (2015) exemplifies the **research trap**: books attracting genuine researchers with credentials and intriguing premises, investing hundreds of pages in technical jargon, then delivering no useful evidence at the conclusion. After 300+ pages of isotope analysis and Cold War anecdotes, Brandenburg concludes: *"No roads or layout of ruins… are visible."* Most remains *"are likely buried under many meters of rubble."* **No photographs. No streets. No artifacts.** The "Face on Mars" is a 3x2 km mound; the "D&M Pyramid" a hill with one area of "collapsed brickwork."

Brandenburg anchors his event at ~180 million years ago — **unfalsifiable**, irrelevant to New Chronology or Saturnian frameworks, compatible with mainstream dating. His DIA contacts told him: *"Why don't you publish?"* — managed disclosure. The book was **allowed** precisely because it leads nowhere. See: [The Mars Catastrophe](/timeline/evt-mars-catastrophe-brandenburg).

## Pre-Brandenburg Mars Programming

Mars fiction *before* Brandenburg prepared audiences: **Rocketship X-M** (1950) — dead civilisation with Geiger counters registering nuclear war; **The Man Who Fell to Earth** (1963/1976) — humanoid refugee from nuclear-devastated planet; **Star Wars** (1977) — Brandenburg explicitly compared Mars to Alderaan. Brandenburg catalogued these films without recognising he was participating in the same pattern.

## Harry Potter and The Matrix

**Harry Potter** functions as mass-initiation propaganda: Hogwarts = boarding school trauma + occult initiation; "Muggles" = humanity taught its inherent power is fiction. **The Matrix** (1999) presents "resistance" as prepackaged control — the "red pill" suggests awakening is binary, and Zion creates the illusion of freedom within the system.

## Latin in SF / Stargate / AI Takeover

**Latin** consistently appears in SF as the language of beings "from above" — encoding the [Deep State's operational language](/timeline/evt-latin-is-the-language-of-the-deep-state). **Stargate** (1994) mythifies pyramid function as "ancient aliens," permanently capturing the topic as "conspiracy theory." **AI takeover narratives** (*Terminator*, *Wall-E*, *I, Robot*) may project what has already happened.

Pre-9/11 media depicting Twin Towers destruction: *The Simpsons* (1997), *The Matrix* (1999 — Neo's passport expires September 11, 2001), numerous album covers. H.G. ## The Counter-Pattern

The Pyramidal Empire's reliance on "Revelation of the Method" has backfired. A population conditioned to recognise predictive programming processes disclosure as **warning**, not consent. Pandemic simulations (Event 201, 2019) were openly discussed before COVID-19; climate emergency declarations follow the same template as 1666 apocalypse prophecies. The control tool has become a signal intelligence channel.
