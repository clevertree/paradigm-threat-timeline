# Predictive Programming: Fiction as Control

![Predictive Programming](/media/15.ce-21st-the-final-struggle/predictive-programming.png)

**Predictive programming** embeds historical truths or planned events into entertainment so audiences fail to recognise reality or dismiss it as coincidence. It operates in two directions: **forward programming** (announcing planned events) and **backward programming** (reframing actual events as myth). This article focuses on backward programming.

## The Wizard of Oz: Atlantis as Children's Entertainment

The 1900 novel and [1939 film](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Wizard_of_Oz_(1939_film)) encode the structure of [Atlantis](/timeline/evt-atlantis-and-the-tree-of-knowledge): **Emerald City** = Poseida (crystal capital); **Yellow Brick Road** = the Bifröst plasma bridge; **Wizard behind the curtain** = the priest class hoarding knowledge. "Pay no attention to the man behind the curtain" presents authority as theatrical.

## Giants: Memory Converted to Fairy Tale

Eliminating giant populations required eliminating memory of them — converting encounters into **children's stories** framing giants as monsters: *Jack the Giant Killer* (1711), *Jack and the Beanstalk* (1807 — 32 years after Pugachev's defeat), *The BFG*, *Attack on Titan*, Harry Potter's Frost Giants. ## Death on Mars: The Research Trap

Brandenburg's *Death on Mars* (2015) exemplifies the **research trap**: books attracting genuine researchers with credentials and intriguing premises, investing hundreds of pages in technical jargon, then delivering no useful evidence at the conclusion. After 300+ pages of isotope analysis and Cold War anecdotes, Brandenburg concludes: *"No roads or layout of ruins… are visible."* Most remains *"are likely buried under many meters of rubble."* **No photographs. No streets. No artifacts.** The "Face on Mars" is a 3x2 km mound; the "D&M Pyramid" a hill with one area of "collapsed brickwork."

Brandenburg anchors his event at ~180 million years ago — **unfalsifiable**, irrelevant to New Chronology or Saturnian frameworks, compatible with mainstream dating. His DIA contacts told him: *"Why don't you publish?"* — managed disclosure. The book was **allowed** precisely because it leads nowhere. See: [The Mars Catastrophe](/timeline/evt-mars-catastrophe-brandenburg).

Mars fiction *before* Brandenburg prepared audiences: **Rocketship X-M** (1950) — dead civilisation with Geiger counters registering nuclear war; **The Man Who Fell to Earth** (1963/1976) — humanoid refugee from nuclear-devastated planet; **Star Wars** (1977) — Brandenburg explicitly compared Mars to Alderaan.
