## Moor Involvement

The Moors (Barbary states) were heavily involved in slavery. Barbary corsairs captured Europeans from Italy, Spain, Portugal, France, Britain, Ireland — raids as far as Iceland. Robert Davis (OSU): 1–1.25M European Christians enslaved 16th–mid-18th c.

**Etymology hypothesis:** "Moor" may have been perceived as "People of Death" (Latin *mor*) due to slave-raiding — folk etymology, not formal derivation. Mainstream: *Maurus* from Mauretania; Phoenician *Mahurin* = "Westerners."
