# Pyramids: Myths vs Reality — Evidence Table (Extended)

This appendix extends [Pyramids: Myths vs Reality](/timeline/evt-pyramids-myths-vs-reality). Detailed evidence for each myth rebuttal.

---

## Myth: Pyramids Are Tombs — Evidence

No mummy ever found in the Great Pyramid. The "sarcophagus" in the King's Chamber has no inscriptions, images, or decoration — unlike every confirmed Egyptian burial. Al-Ma'mun's men (820 CE) reportedly found not a body but a reservoir with gold coins ([Fomenko, HiW §4.13](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/04_10.html)). Worst-designed tomb ever: no burial goods, no identification, no remains.

## Myth: Slaves Dragged Blocks — Evidence

No ancient depictions of block-dragging construction. No ramp remnants at Giza. Block precision (sub-millimetre) inconsistent with sledge-hauling. **Joseph Davidovich**: blocks are geopolymeric concrete — cast in moulds from ground limestone, Nile clay (aluminium oxide), natron, water ([*The Pyramids: An Enigma Solved*](https://www.geopolymer.org)). Matting marks from casting visible above 50 m. See [Geopolymer Concrete](/timeline/evt-geopolymer-concrete-horde-secret).

## Myth: Bronze-Age Technology — Evidence

Steel tools embedded in Khufu masonry. Herodotus: iron tools used. Hack-saw marks on internal joists show steel blade teeth — impossible with copper or bronze.

## Myth: Hand-Carved Diorite Vessels — Evidence

Diorite harder than iron; vessels have narrow necks, uniform wall thickness, no drill/chisel marks. Davidovich: cast on potter's wheel using geopolymeric concrete. Nefertiti head: visible **mould seam** — proof of casting, not carving.
