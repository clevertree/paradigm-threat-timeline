# Historical Antibodies: Independent Agents Against Erasure

Antibodies are independent agents who preserved suppressed knowledge or exposed covert operations — not [controlled opposition](/timeline/evt-controlled-opposition). Examples: C.S. Lewis (encoded planetary cosmology in *Narnia*), Daniel Ellsberg, Giordano Bruno, Smedley Butler, Nikola Tesla, Velikovsky, Gary Webb, William Cooper. The defining characteristic is **asymmetry**: a single person permanently alters the information landscape.
