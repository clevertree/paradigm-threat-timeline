# 20th C. Predictive Programming

The decades following [Operation Mockingbird](/timeline/evt-operation-mockingbird) saw the industrialisation of a technique far older than the CIA: **reframing real events as fiction**. From the 1890s onward, authors — some conscious operatives, some cultural carriers — systematically converted suppressed history into "fantasy" and "science fiction." The effect was **erasure**. Once a real event becomes a novel, anyone who later discovers the truth sounds like they are describing a plot summary.

This is the **backward** direction of [predictive programming](/timeline/evt-predictive-programming): not announcing future operations, but burying past ones under copyright-protected fiction.

**Wells** — Crystal Egg (scrying), War of the Worlds (heat ray, red weed), Plattner/Davidson (dimensional, remote viewing). **Machen** — *Great God Pan* (brain surgery → dimensional perception); *Angels of Mons* (fiction as news). **Blackwood** — *The Willows*, *John Silence* (thought as surveillance). **Lovecraft** — 104 stories encoding scrying, remote viewing, telepathic broadcast. **Burroughs** — Barsoom: polar conspiracies, telepathic tech. **Tolkien** — Red Book pseudotranslation; Silmarillion = systematic reassembly; copyright until 2043+. **Card** — *Ender's Game*: planetary destruction via "simulations," Marine Corps Reading List.

**The pattern:** Each author converts suppressed knowledge into protected fiction. Wells (1890s) = Hordian erasure; Lovecraft (interwar) = Mockingbird consolidation; Tolkien = CIA formalisation; Card = Cold War peak.
