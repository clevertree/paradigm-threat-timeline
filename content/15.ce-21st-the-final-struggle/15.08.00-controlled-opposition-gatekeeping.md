# Controlled Opposition: Managed Disclosure and Gatekeeping

Controlled opposition channels dissent into safe boundaries: forbidden ideas surface only under management. The pattern — attract seekers, establish authority, draw a boundary, suppress internal dissent, absorb and neutralise — operates through organisations, genre classification, and platform control.
