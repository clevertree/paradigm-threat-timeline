# 20th Century Predictive Programming: Literature as Historical Erasure

The decades following [Operation Mockingbird](/timeline/evt-operation-mockingbird) saw the industrialisation of a technique far older than the CIA: **reframing real events as fiction**. From the 1890s onward, a constellation of authors — some conscious operatives, some cultural carriers — systematically converted suppressed history into "fantasy" and "science fiction." The effect was not entertainment. It was **erasure**. Once a real event becomes a novel, anyone who later discovers the truth sounds like they are describing a plot summary.

This is the **backward** direction of [predictive programming](/timeline/evt-predictive-programming): not announcing future operations, but burying past ones under copyright-protected fiction.

## H.G. Wells (1866–1946): The Template

Wells set the pattern. His **Crystal Egg** (1897) describes a crystal sphere that provides real-time visual surveillance of Mars — an observer on Earth watches Martians through it, and the Martians watch back through a matching device. Wells wrote: the crystal was *"sent hither from that planet, in order to give the Martians a near view of our affairs."* This is not a metaphor. It is a description of a two-way scrying device identical to Tolkien's later Palantíri and to the remote-viewing technology declassified under the [Stargate programme](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stargate_Project) in 1995. Tolkien makes the arc explicit: his Palantíri *"long guarded and united the realm of Gondor"* until Sauron perverted them for subversion. Wells shows only the hostile phase — Martian surveillance for invasion. Same technology, same trajectory: **guard a realm → use for evil**.

*The War of the Worlds* (1898) inverted prior Mars fiction — which portrayed Martians as benign — into an invasion narrative with a **heat ray** matching the U.S. military's later [Active Denial System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Active_Denial_System). Wells described it as an *invisible* beam generated in "a chamber of practically absolute non-conductivity" and projected by a parabolic mirror—a realistic capacitor-driven directed-energy weapon. Most WotW adaptations changed it to a visible sci-fi laser and dropped the technical specifics. The 2017 authorised sequel (*The Massacre of Mankind*) is one of only two versions (with Jeff Wayne's 1978 album) to keep it invisible—published one year after Havana syndrome (2016) was attributed to directed-energy weapons. See `wget/wells/massacre-of-mankind-2017-reference.md` for the full investigation. Wells's **red weed** appeared six years before the [American chestnut blight](/timeline/evt-american-chestnut-blight) killed four billion trees. His *War in the Air* (1908) described aerial bombing of cities a decade before it happened. His *The World Set Free* (1914) coined the term "atomic bomb" thirty years before Hiroshima.

Wells was not guessing. He was a Fabian Society insider with intelligence connections, and [Florence Deeks demonstrated](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Florence_Deeks) he plagiarised her unpublished manuscript for *The Outline of History*. His function was to publish real knowledge — past events and planned operations — as fiction so that the general public would treat the information as already-known entertainment rather than disclosure.

### The Plattner Story (1896) and Davidson's Eyes (1895)

*The Plattner Story* sends a chemistry teacher into a **fourth spatial dimension** through a chemical explosion, then returns him with his body **mirror-reversed** (heart on the right side) — physical proof of dimensional displacement. *Davidson's Eyes* describes spontaneous remote viewing: a man in London sees in real-time through the eyes of a lighthouse keeper on a South Sea island. Both stories normalise interdimensional and remote-viewing phenomena as fantasy a century before the U.S. government admitted researching them.

## Arthur Machen (1863–1947): Brain Surgery and Angels

**Machen** (Golden Dawn): *Great God Pan* (1894) — brain surgery opens dimensional perception; patient sees beyond the veil, goes insane. Lovecraft's *From Beyond*: same mechanism, electronic. *Angels of Mons* (1915): fiction published as news; public believed it true — documented fiction→reality; the reverse (fact→fiction) works the same way.

## Algernon Blackwood (1869–1951): Thought as Surveillance

**Blackwood** *The Willows* (1907): entities detect humans through thought; geographic veils. *John Silence* (1908): occult intelligence operative — physician detecting non-human influence. Encodes: such knowledge is real, learnable, operational — filed as fiction.

## H.P. Lovecraft (1890–1937): Cosmic Horror as Disclosure

Lovecraft's 104 stories encode suppressed knowledge: Shining Trapezohedron (scrying crystal = Wells/Tolkien); resonator (*From Beyond*, pineal activation); Great Race of Yith (remote viewing across time); Mi-Go (consciousness in metal cylinders); Cthulhu (mass telepathic broadcast). Framed as cosmic horror: *do not investigate; the truth is madness*. For full device catalog, see [Appendix: Predictive Programming Film, TV, Games](/timeline/evt-pp-film-tv-games).

## Edgar Rice Burroughs (1875–1950): The Unmatched Source

Barsoom (1912–43): polar conspiracies, religious fraud, telepathic tech, Kaldane/Rykor, Ras Thavas — detail Lowell could not supply. Burroughs: no known intel links; failed businessman at 36. Possibility: suppressed sources or recording tech, pitched as fiction. Same mechanism as Crystal Egg, Palantíri, seer stones. ## J.R.R. Tolkien (1892–1973): The Most Protected Operation

Tolkien as translator: Red Book of Westmarch = pseudotranslation; he insisted *recovery*, not invention. Red Book modeled on [Red Book of Hergest](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Book_of_Hergest) (Mabinogion); he forged physical artifacts (McIlwaine 2018). **Silmarillion** = systematic reassembly: Ainulindalë→Kalevala, Gondolin→Troy+Ragnarök, One Ring→Andvari. **Dvergatal:** Thorin, Gandalf, Oakenshield verbatim from Elder Edda. Snorri: gods = real kings from Troy. *Lost Road*, *Notion Club Papers*: academics discover myths are real. Fomenko parallel: Shakespeare/Tolkien = literary reworking of Rus-Horde history. Copyright until 2043+ shields from analysis. See [Predictive Programming Appendix](/timeline/evt-predictive-programming) for Tolkien source tables.

## Orson Scott Card (b. 1951): The Mormon Channel

Card (LDS, great-great-grandson of Brigham Young) published *Ender's Game* in 1985: a child unknowingly commands planetary destruction via "simulations" that are real battles; telepathic hive-mind enemy; secondary discharge destroys the attacker's fleet; dictator rises via media manipulation; truth told as fiction. The U.S. Marine Corps placed it on their Reading List. Drone warfare replicates the scenario. Card's novel functions as predictive programming, training manual, and encoded history — the [Mars catastrophe](/timeline/evt-mars-catastrophe-brandenburg) retold. The pattern (planet attacks planet, suffers blow-back) appears in Wells, Lovecraft, Lucas, Hubbard, *Stargate* — suggesting a **common source**.

**The pattern:** Each author converts suppressed knowledge into protected fiction. Wells (1890s) = Hordian erasure; Lovecraft (interwar) = Mockingbird consolidation; Tolkien = CIA formalisation; Card = Cold War peak. For full author table: [Predictive Programming Appendix](/timeline/evt-predictive-programming). Wells begins publishing in the 1890s — the decade when the Hordian Empire's final traces are being overwritten. Lovecraft writes through the interwar period when Mockingbird's predecessors are consolidating control. Tolkien publishes as the CIA formalises Mockingbird. Card publishes as the Cold War peaks and drone warfare begins development.

Each author converts a specific category of suppressed knowledge into a specific genre of protected fiction. The cumulative effect: anyone who discovers the real events — remote viewing, Mars civilisation, planetary weapons, telepathic species, dimensional perception — is told they are describing **a novel they read**.

**Copyright functions as classification:** Tolkien until 2043+; Lovecraft public domain 2007–32; Card under active copyright.

## Film, Television, and Games

Literature established the templates; film and games **scaled** them to mass audiences. Mars as dead planet, instant teleportation, telepathic purges, AI takeover, zombie apocalypse, transhuman occupation — these themes repeat across hundreds of productions with a consistency that defies coincidence. *Half-Life 2* (2004) may be the single most accurate depiction of interplanetary technocratic occupation in any medium: the Combine, instant portals, resource extraction, reproductive suppression, communism as imposed religion, and biological zombification — all in one coherent narrative.
