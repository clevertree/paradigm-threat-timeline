# 1898–2026 CE — False Flags: Architecture of Manufactured War

A false flag is an operation designed to appear as if carried out by someone other than its actual perpetrators. The pattern: spectacular attack → blame assigned within hours → pre-drafted legislation executed. Pearl Harbor (McCollum Memo, Purple code broken, carriers at sea), Gulf of Tonkin, 9/11.
