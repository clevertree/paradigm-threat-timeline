# Predictive Programming: Fiction as Control

![Predictive Programming](/media/15.ce-21st-the-final-struggle/predictive-programming.png)

**Predictive programming** embeds historical truths or planned events into entertainment so audiences either fail to recognize reality or dismiss it as coincidence. This operates in two directions: **forward programming** (announcing planned events) and **backward programming** (reframing actual events as myth). This article focuses on backward programming — converting ancient testimony into entertainment.

The 1900 novel [The Wonderful Wizard of Oz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Wonderful_Wizard_of_Oz) and [1939 film](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Wizard_of_Oz_(1939_film)) encode the structure of [Atlantis](/content/02.the-golden-age/02.07.00-atlantis-and-the-tree-of-knowledge.md): **Emerald City** = Poseida (crystal capital); **Yellow Brick Road** = the Bifröst plasma bridge; **Wizard behind the curtain** = the priest class operating temple technology. "Pay no attention to the man behind the curtain" reveals authority as theatrical and knowledge as hoarded.

## War of the Worlds: Real Event as Fiction

H.G. Wells [demonstrably plagiarized](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Florence_Deeks) Florence Deeks' work. His **heat ray** matches the U.S. Military's [Active Denial System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Active_Denial_System). Before Wells, Mars fiction portrayed Martians as benign (Percy Greg's *Across the Zodiac*, 1880); Wells deliberately inverted this. [Orson Welles' 1938 broadcast](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_War_of_the_Worlds_(1938_radio_drama)) used "Revelation of the Method" — declaring "this is fiction" while broadcasting realistic reports.

In 2015, plasma physicist **John E. Brandenburg** published *Death on Mars* proving isotopically that Mars suffered two massive nuclear airbursts — the xenon-129 superabundance is hydrogen bomb detonation's fingerprint. The explosion centers correspond to Cydonia and Galaxias Chaos archaeological sites. Brandenburg confirms what Wells encoded as fiction. See [The Mars Catastrophe](/content/12.ce-18th-mudflood-and-pugachev/12.06.00-1774-ce-the-mars-catastrophe-brandenburgs-nuclear-evidence.md).

Wells's **red weed** — a "vivid blood-red growth" of alien origin that choked native vegetation — appeared six years before the [American chestnut blight](/content/14.ce-20th-the-1917-revolution/14.05.00-1904-ce-the-american-chestnut-blight-martian-fungus-on-earth.md) (1904), an orange-cankered fungus that killed four billion trees.
