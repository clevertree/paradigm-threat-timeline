# Predictive Programming: Fiction as Control

![Predictive Programming](/media/15.ce-21st-the-final-struggle/predictive-programming.png)

**Predictive programming** embeds historical truths or planned events into entertainment so audiences fail to recognize reality or dismiss it as coincidence. This operates in two directions: **forward programming** (announcing planned events) and **backward programming** (reframing actual events as myth). This article focuses on backward programming — converting ancient testimony into entertainment.
