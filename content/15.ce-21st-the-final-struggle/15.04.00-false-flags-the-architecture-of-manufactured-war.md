# False Flags: The Architecture of Manufactured War

> *"In politics, nothing happens by accident. If it happens, you can bet it was planned that way."*
> — Franklin Delano Roosevelt

A **false flag** is a military or political operation designed to appear as if carried out by someone other than its actual perpetrators. The name comes from the naval practice of flying another nation's flag to approach an enemy ship undetected. In the 20th and 21st centuries, false flags became the **primary mechanism** by which the Deep State manufactured public consent for wars, domestic surveillance, and regime change — transforming reluctant democracies into all-out war machines overnight.

The pattern is always the same: **a spectacular attack occurs, blame is assigned within hours, and pre-drafted legislation or military plans are executed before the public can think.** The Gulf of Tonkin. The sinking of the Lusitania. The Reichstag Fire. But none of these compare — in scale, in premeditation, or in consequences — to Pearl Harbor.

## Pearl Harbor: The Mother of All False Flags

On December 7, 1941, Japan attacked the U.S. Pacific Fleet at Pearl Harbor, killing 2,403 Americans. The official narrative says the attack was a surprise. The evidence says otherwise.

### The McCollum Memo

On October 7, 1940 — **fourteen months before the attack** — Lieutenant Commander [Arthur McCollum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/McCollum_memo) of the Office of Naval Intelligence submitted an eight-point memorandum outlining specific actions designed to provoke Japan into an overt act of war. The memo concluded: *"If by these means Japan could be led to commit an overt act of war, so much the better."* All eight actions were subsequently implemented:

1. Arrange use of British bases in the Pacific (Singapore)
2. Arrange use of Dutch East Indies facilities
3. Give all possible aid to Chiang-Kai-Shek
4. Send heavy cruisers to the Orient
5. Send submarine divisions to the Orient
6. Keep the main U.S. fleet in Hawaiian waters
7. Insist the Dutch refuse Japanese oil demands
8. Completely embargo all U.S. trade with Japan

### The Fleet Was Bait

FDR personally ordered the Pacific Fleet relocated from San Diego to Pearl Harbor against the explicit protests of Admiral James O. Richardson, who warned the position was indefensible. Richardson was **relieved of command** for his objections and replaced with Admiral [Husband Kimmel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Husband_E._Kimmel), who positioned the fleet as ordered. Critically, the ships stationed at Pearl were **mostly obsolete battleships**. The aircraft carriers — the most valuable assets — were conveniently at sea on the day of the attack.

### The Codes Were Broken

The U.S. had cracked Japan's [Purple diplomatic code](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Purple_(cipher_machine)) in 1940. Decrypted messages — codenamed "Magic" — were delivered to the Roosevelt administration almost daily. On November 24, 1941, Admiral Yamamoto's order to the strike force was intercepted: *"The task force, keeping its movement strictly secret... shall advance into Hawaiian waters, and upon the very opening of hostilities shall attack the main force of the United States fleet."* This message was **never forwarded** to Kimmel or Short.

Secretary of War Henry Stimson recorded in his diary: *"In spite of the risk involved, however, in letting the Japanese fire the first shot, we realized that in order to have the full support of the American people, it was desirable to make sure that the Japanese be the ones to do this."*

Rear Admiral Frank Beatty, aide to Secretary Knox and close to Roosevelt's inner circle, later stated: *"Prior to December 7, it was evident even to me... that we were pushing Japan into a corner. I believed that it was the desire of President Roosevelt, and Prime Minister Churchill, that we get into the war."*

### The Scapegoats

Kimmel and Short were blamed for the catastrophe and denied court-martial — because a trial would have forced disclosure of the broken codes and intelligence withheld from Hawaii. Admiral Kimmel spent the rest of his life seeking vindication: *"I'll tell you what I believe. I think that most of the incriminating records have been destroyed. I doubt if the truth will ever emerge."* Vice Admiral Libby added: *"I will go to my grave convinced that FDR ordered Pearl Harbor to let happen."*

## The Battle of Los Angeles: What People Actually Saw

Ten weeks after Pearl Harbor, on February 24–25, 1942, the U.S. military fired **over 1,400 anti-aircraft rounds** at unidentified targets over Los Angeles. The Office of Naval Intelligence had warned of an expected Japanese attack within ten hours. Sirens blared at 2:25 AM. Firing continued from 3:16 to 4:14 AM. Five civilians died.

Key facts:
- **No bombs were dropped.** No enemy aircraft were downed. No wreckage was recovered
- Secretary of the Navy Frank Knox called it a **"false alarm"** the next day — contradicting the Army
- **Japan confirmed post-war they flew no aircraft over Los Angeles**
- The Air Force later blamed a "weather balloon" — the same explanation used for [Roswell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roswell_incident) five years later
- The famous LA Times photograph shows searchlights converging on a luminous object that **survived 1,400+ rounds without damage**

Whatever the people of Los Angeles saw that night was not Japanese aircraft. In the heightened atmosphere of post-Pearl-Harbor paranoia, thousands of witnesses saw something in the sky that the military could not shoot down and the government could not explain. The incident has been absorbed into UFO history, but its timing — immediately after the most significant false flag in American history — is not coincidental.
