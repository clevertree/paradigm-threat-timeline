# Population Control: From 1492 to the Technocratic Grid

The Georgia Guidestones, erected in 1980 in rural Georgia, inscribed in eight languages a directive to "maintain humanity under 500,000,000 in perpetual balance with nature." The stones were destroyed in 2022 — but the programme they announced has operated for centuries. Population control is the recurring Deep State mechanism to periodically reduce human numbers, restore order, or engineer cultural revolution when populations exceed manageability.

The pattern predates the Guidestones by half a millennium. For the full chronological dossier from 1492 through the 21st century, see [Population Control: A Timeline (15th–21st Centuries)](/timeline/evt-population-control-timeline) in the Appendix.

## Modern Forms: Suppression and Poisoning

Research into [Tennant](/timeline/evt-ap-jerry-tennant) and the [Flexner Report](/timeline/evt-flexner-bioelectric-suppression) demonstrates that Rockefeller and allied foundations systematically suppressed health, medicine, and science that could extend life and cure chronic disease. The mechanism: defund non-compliant schools, criminalize electrotherapy, enforce drug-and-surgery-only paradigms. The result — generations denied bioelectric healing, nutrition-based recovery, and energy medicine — constitutes **suppressed science as population control**.

Direct poisoning operates in parallel: adulterated food, environmental pollution, questionable vaccine mandates, and harmful radiation (from medical over-imaging to EMF saturation) produce attrition without declared war. Food shortage and engineered starvation, collapse of bee populations and ecosystem degradation, solar-dimming proposals to block crops, and herbicide spraying that kills or contaminates harvests — the Gaza conflict exemplifies attrition through denial of food, water, and medicine. All produce population reduction under plausible deniability.

## Body and Family: Making Resistance Impossible

Modern population control extends beyond death into **dependency and surrender of reproduction**. People are encouraged to forgo families, voluntarily sterilize, and treat children as burdens. Body modification — piercings, tattoos, surgical augmentation, and emerging chip/hardware implants — produces a more controllable society: populations bound to master technology, like the lower classes in H.G. Wells's *War of the Worlds*, reduced to servile dependency on those who control the machines.

The end goal: a point where resistance is not only impossible but **unthinkable**. A population that has given up sovereignty, reproduction, and bodily integrity cannot mount effective opposition.

## Children of Men: Predictive Programming

Alfonso Cuarón's *Children of Men* (2006) encodes population control as predictive programming. Global infertility strikes without explanation; everyone agrees "God" is punishing humanity. The government bunkers down, collects art from around the world — and the public never suspects the government is behind the infertility. The film trains audiences to accept a future where population collapse is inevitable and unquestioned, where authority consolidates while citizens despair. Predictive programming prepares populations for predicted events by depicting them as fiction first — so when the real event arrives, the response is already programmed.
