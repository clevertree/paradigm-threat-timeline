# 2009 CE: CERN's LHC and the Portal — Aether Manipulation in the Open

In November 2009, Sergio Bertolucci — CERN's Director for Research and Scientific Computing — briefed reporters at CERN HQ on the Large Hadron Collider. He stated that the LHC might create or discover "previously unimagined scientific phenomena," including "an extra dimension," and said:

> **"Out of this door might come something, or we might send something through it."**

The words are on the record.
