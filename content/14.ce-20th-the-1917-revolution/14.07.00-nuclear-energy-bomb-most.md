# Nuclear Energy & "Bomb": Most

## The Official Story

In 1938, Otto Hahn and Fritz Strassmann [discovered nuclear fission](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discovery_of_nuclear_fission) — the splitting of a uranium atom into lighter elements, releasing enormous energy. By 1942, the [Manhattan Project](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manhattan_Project) had marshalled 130,000 workers and $2 billion (≈$28 billion today) to weaponize this discovery. On 16 July 1945, the Trinity test detonated the first nuclear device in the New Mexico desert. Three weeks later, "Little Boy" and "Fat Man" were dropped on Hiroshima and Nagasaki, killing an estimated 100,000–200,000 people and ending World War II. The world entered the Atomic Age. The Cold War arms race followed, with the United States and Soviet Union each stockpiling tens of thousands of warheads capable of ending civilization — or so the story goes.

**Timeline:** Becquerel (1896) → Hahn/Strassmann (1938) → Manhattan Project (1942) → Chicago Pile-1, Hanford B Reactor (1944) → Trinity, Hiroshima, Nagasaki (1945) → AEC (1947) → Ford/Carter reprocessing ban (1976–77). The physics is real; what is fabricated is the **story** — nuclear material as uniquely dangerous, civilization-ending bombs, unsolvable "waste." For full chronology see [Suppressed Energy Cross-Reference](/timeline/evt-suppressed-energy-technology-cross-reference).

**Galen Windsor** (1926–2008), Hanford fuel-handling engineer, [lectured](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=galen+windsor+nuclear+scare+scam) that reactors are steam plants; he handled enriched fuel with bare hands, swam in spent-fuel pools, ate radioactive material on camera. [Harold McCluskey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harold_McCluskey) survived 500× americium exposure, no cancer at autopsy. Enriched material is far less dangerous than the public has been told.

**Spent fuel vs. depleted uranium.** Spent fuel = $10M/ton reprocessable inventory; France, UK, Russia, Japan reprocess. The US banned reprocessing (Ford 1976, Carter 1977); 772 tons sit at [Morris](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morris_Operation), tens of thousands more in pools. [Depleted uranium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Depleted_uranium) (DU) is the opposite — 315–350 tons used in Gulf War munitions; Fallujah shows catastrophic genetic damage; veterans 14× chromosome abnormalities. The narrative is inverted: enriched = "lethally dangerous" (Windsor ate it); DU = "mildly radioactive" (actually harmful).

**Hiroshima/Nagasaki.** Damage pattern matches firebombing (Tokyo March 1945); vegetation returned in weeks; "shadow" imprints consistent with phosphorus flash effects; 15–21 kt yield a post-hoc claim. Test footage analysis: models, compositing, stacked dynamite (Eric Dubay, *Nuclear Hoax*, 2019). Plausible alternative: small DU device + incendiary powder.

**Money machine.** Manhattan $2B (≈$28B today); Cold War trillions; evidence classified. Windsor: "siphon off money, keep us afraid." Same architecture as [weather control](/timeline/evt-weather-control) and [Flexner's medical suppression](/timeline/evt-flexner-bioelectric-suppression).

The nuclear narrative is about **control** — energy policy, public fear, military spending — not physics. See [Suppressed Energy Cross-Reference](/timeline/evt-suppressed-energy-technology-cross-reference) for the full pattern.
