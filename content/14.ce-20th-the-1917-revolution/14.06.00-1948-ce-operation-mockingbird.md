# Operation Mockingbird: State-Sponsored Erasure Goes Public

![Operation Mockingbird](/media/14.ce-20th-the-1917-revolution/mockingbird.png)

## The Official Story

[Operation Mockingbird](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Mockingbird) was a CIA program beginning in the late 1940s to influence domestic and foreign media. Journalists at major outlets — *The Washington Post*, *Time*, *Newsweek*, CBS — were recruited or paid to publish stories aligned with U.S. intelligence objectives. The program was partially exposed during the [Church Committee hearings](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Church_Committee) (1975) and is officially said to have ended. The name came from the mockingbird's talent: imitating the songs of other birds — a creature that produces no original sound, only convincing copies.

## A Small Link in a Long Chain

Mockingbird was not an innovation. It was the **state sponsorship** of a program that had been running for centuries under different names and different patrons.

### The Ship of Fools: Mockingbird's Reformation Prototype

The earliest documented version of the Mockingbird program was the Reformation-era propaganda campaign Fomenko calls **"The Ship of Fools"** ([chronologia.org Ch. 8 §21](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/08_21.html)). In 1494, Sebastian Brant published *The Ship of Fools* — illustrated by Dürer himself — depicting a vessel crewed by idiots sailing toward oblivion. The book was a sensation, reprinted endlessly, translated across Europe. Fomenko identifies what it actually was: **a coordinated propaganda assault on the Hordian Empire**.

> *"It is clear now what 'fools' were held up to ridicule. The Great Empire, its institutions, its Christian Orthodox Faith, the Cossack = Israeli troops — which still had garrisons in Europe of XVI–XVII cc. The rebels and provocateurs still feared them. That is why they veiled the satire without pointing directly at the target of their abuse."*

The method was identical to Mockingbird's: recruit talented artists and writers, fund them generously, and produce content that **appears to mock "stupidity in general"** while actually targeting a specific political enemy. When challenged, the authors could claim innocence — *"We didn't have anything like that in mind. This is the way we castigate 'fools in general.'"* The CIA's journalists used precisely the same plausible deniability four centuries later.

The Ship of Fools eventually became a symbol of **Hell itself**. The reformers staged carnival processions where the Ship was literally **stormed and destroyed** by crowds — a rehearsal for the collapse they were engineering. Massive funds were poured into these spectacles: directors, artists, actors, and politicians were summoned to produce lavish "show-lessons." As Fomenko writes: *"The reformers were open and didn't hide the purpose of the propaganda which was to take up arms and destroy the 'Mongol' Empire."*

Martin Luther — whom Fomenko argues was likely a **supporter** of the Empire, his reputation later hijacked by the reformers — condemned the 1539 Schembartlauf carnival as *"a particularly ungodly show expressing disregard to the Gospels."* His protest was ignored. After the Empire collapsed, the carnivals were abolished — their work done. The weapon was sent to a museum and vaguely remembered as "sweet mischiefs and glory."

## The Program Never Ended

The Church Committee's exposure created a useful fiction: that because Mockingbird was "revealed," it must have stopped. This is the classic **Revelation of the Method** — disclosing a program to inoculate the public against believing it continues. The CIA's formal relationship with specific journalists may have been restructured, but the operational logic — embedding narrative control into media at the point of production — migrated into new vehicles:

- **Cable news** (CNN founded 1980, Fox News 1996, MSNBC 1996) — consolidating all broadcast narrative into a handful of controlled outlets
- **Hollywood studio system** — where CIA and Pentagon "entertainment liaison offices" review scripts in exchange for access to military hardware and locations
- **Social media algorithms** — replacing human editors with automated systems that achieve the same censorship at scale, without requiring individual journalist recruitment
- **Fact-checking organizations** — creating a new class of narrative gatekeepers who enforce consensus by labeling dissent as "misinformation"

The name changes. The function does not.

## The Mockingbird's Song

The choice of the mockingbird as symbol was more revealing than the CIA intended. A mockingbird does not create — it **imitates**. It takes the authentic songs of other birds and reproduces them so convincingly that listeners cannot distinguish copy from original. This is precisely what the program does to history: it takes authentic events, repackages them as fiction or "conspiracy theory," and replaces the original with a copy so pervasive that the real thing sounds false. The mockingbird sings loudest at night — when no one can see what kind of bird is actually making the sound.

Mockingbird's most critical function was maintaining the cover stories for [false flag operations](/timeline/evt-false-flags) — from Pearl Harbor to the Gulf of Tonkin to 9/11. Without a controlled media apparatus, no false flag can survive public scrutiny for more than a few days. With it, false narratives can be sustained for generations.
