# Operation Mockingbird: State-Sponsored Erasure Goes Public

## The Official Story

[Operation Mockingbird](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Mockingbird) was a CIA program from the late 1940s to influence domestic and foreign media. Journalists at *The Washington Post*, *Time*, *Newsweek*, CBS were recruited or paid to publish stories aligned with U.S. intelligence. Partially exposed at the [Church Committee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Church_Committee) (1975); officially ended. The mockingbird imitates other birds — produces no original sound, only convincing copies.

Ship of Fools (1494), Protestant fracturing of Hordian Empire, Romanov redaction, Scaligerian chronology, Soviet falsification — at each stage: control the story, ridicule alternatives, punish dissent. ## The Program Never Ended

The Church Committee exposure created a fiction: that because Mockingbird was "revealed," it must have stopped. **Revelation of the Method.** The operational logic migrated: cable news (CNN, Fox, MSNBC), Hollywood liaison offices, social media algorithms, fact-checking organizations. The name changes; the function does not.
