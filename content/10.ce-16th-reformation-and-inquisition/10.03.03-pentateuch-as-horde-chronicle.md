# The Pentateuch as a Horde Chronicle

Fomenko and Nosovskiy make one of their most striking claims in [Chapter 8, §7](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/08_07.html) of *How It Was In Reality*:

> *"The Hordians wrote well and a lot. For example, they created the significant part of the Old Testament including the Pentateuch. But in the epoch of the Time of Troubles and the Romanovs pogrom-occupation they could not hold on to the major segments of their history. They took it away from us and attributed it to others. Instead they invented for us and bequeathed to us the 'gloomy Tatar yoke'. Which is still taught at school."*

If the Pentateuch (Genesis–Deuteronomy) was composed by Hordian authors in the XV–XVI centuries, then "ancient Israelites" are the Rus-Horde Empire itself, and biblical "ancient history" is medieval Imperial history displaced thousands of years into the past.

## What the Claim Means

In New Chronology, Old Testament books were composed in the XV–XVII centuries, drawing on real events and geography, then pushed backwards in time:

- **Genesis** — founding mythology of the Empire
- **Exodus** — reflects Ottoman/Horde XV century conquests (see [The Reverse Exodus Theory](/timeline/evt-the-reverse-exodus-theory))
- **Leviticus / Numbers** — priestly law and census records of Empire administration
- **Deuteronomy** — post-schism codification of religious law

When Romanovs took power in the XVII century, these documents were seized, attributed to "ancient Hebrews," and dated thousands of years earlier.

## The Book of Judges as Direct Evidence

Fomenko identifies **Book of Judges** as a direct account of the [XVI century Inquisition](/timeline/evt-the-holy-inquisition) in Western Europe:

- **Deborah** = [Joan of Arc](/timeline/evt-jeanne-darc-is-executed-in-rouen-france)
- **Abimelech** = Simon de Montfort, who led the [Cathar crusade](/timeline/evt-cathar-suppression)
- **Samson** = Gilles de Rais

The same events, written in Hebrew and French, later separated into "biblical" and "medieval" history by chronological displacement.

## The Books of Esther and Judith

The [Books of Esther and Judith](/timeline/evt-redacted-into-the-books-of-esther-judith) are Hordian accounts of the XVI century [Oprichnina coup](/timeline/evt-romanov-dynasty-stages-the-oprichnina-coup) — Zakharyin-Romanov infiltration of the Russian court. Elena Voloshanka = Biblical Esther; "massacre of the Persians" = Oprichnina massacre. Written in XVI century Russia, then confiscated, relabelled as ancient scripture, embedded in fabricated chronology.

## The Romanov Erasure

1. **Confiscation** — After the Time of Troubles (1598–1613), Romanovs seized Hordian archives
2. **Attribution** — Texts reattributed to "ancient Hebrews," dated per Scaligerian chronology
3. **Replacement** — "Tatar yoke" narrative invented, casting the Empire's founders as foreign invaders
4. **Destruction** — Documents that could not be repurposed were destroyed

The Hordians lost both history and authorship. Their greatest literary achievement — the Pentateuch — was attributed to phantom "ancient" people, while they were rebranded as illiterate nomads.
