# Khazar Rebellion in the Russia-Horde Empire

In the mid-16th century the **Kazan kingdom** — identified by Fomenko as the [Khazar Khaganate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khazar_Khaganate) — became the centre of a Judaic religious movement. The Kazan czar converted to Judaism and attempted to break from the [Russia-Horde Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mongol_Empire). In 1552 czar-khan Ivan the "Terrible" crushed the revolt.

The name Khazar is also a transliteration of Katya/Katherine. Annihilation of the Khazars and [Cathars](/timeline/evt-cathar-suppression) effectively ended the original Christian revolution — both movements went underground, heavily redacted. Khazarian Judaism differed from modern Judaism: the Messiah, Christ i.e. King Solomon the [Sun King](https://www.solarancestor.com/regular-blog-posts/-sol-om-on-a-sun-king), had *already* arrived.

## Kazan = The Khazar Khaganate

Mainstream consensus places the Khaganate in the 7th–10th centuries. The [New Chronology](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/06_01.html) rejects this: Kazan and the Khaganate are the same polity — both centred on the middle Volga, ruled by a Kagan/Khan, known for court conversion to Judaism, attempted independence from the northern power, violently suppressed by Rus. The "ancient" Khaganate is a phantom shifted ~600 years earlier.

## What "Judaism" and "Israel" Actually Meant

> *"The Kazan Judaism of the XVI century and contemporary Judaism should not be confused. The present names of the religions originated from positive sounding notions. For instance, Jew meant 'One who praises God'."*
> — *How It Was In Reality*, Ch. 6, §1

In the 14th–16th centuries: **Israel** = "Fighter for God," the entire Russia-Horde Empire. **Judaea** = Ottoman Empire. **Jew** = "One who praises God," a positive epithet. Judaism split from unified Royal Christianity — the Kazan conversion was a *religious secession within the Empire's own tradition*.

## The Conquest of Kazan (1552) and Its Encoded Doubles

Ivan's army built **Sviyazhsk** as a forward base before crossing the Volga. Classical sources encode this as: Herodotus' King **Cambyses** conquering "Egyptian Memphis" (= Kazan), and King **Xerxes** building a bridge across the Hellespont (= Sviyazhsk/Volga crossing) for a punitive campaign against "disobedient Greeks" (= the rebellious Khanate).

## Connection to the Western Reformation

The Kazan rebellion coincided with Western European governors using Lutheranism as their secession banner — called the *heresy of the Judaizers* in Russia. Both shared: religious separation from the Empire's Orthodox metropoly, links to Judaizer networks inside the court, simultaneous mid-16th century timing. The political class weaponising Lutheranism for secession was the **Zakharyins-Romanovs**.

## After Kazan: The Livonian War

Having crushed Kazan, Russia-Horde launched the **Livonian War** to suppress the Western Reformation — permanently aborted by the **[Zakharyin-Romanov coup](/timeline/evt-romanov-dynasty-stages-the-oprichnina-coup)**.

## Velikovsky: The Khazars as Lost Tribes

Velikovsky's unpublished *Beyond the Mountains of Darkness* concluded independently that the Khazars descended from the **Ten Lost Tribes**, exiled to the Volga. Hebrew was state language. Khazar kings bore biblical names (Hezekiah, Manasseh, Obadiah). "Khazars" derives from Hebrew "Those Who Return." Geographic names confirm: Kazan from Gozan (exile river), Samara from Samaria. Where Fomenko identifies chronological displacement, Velikovsky identifies ethnic continuity.
