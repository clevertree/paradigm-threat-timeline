The very names Constantinople and Constantine carry this lie in their root: Latin **constans**, meaning "standing firm, unchanging, constant."*

## The Doctrine of Constancy

When Deep State [rebuilt Jerusalem at Istanbul](/timeline?event=evt-deep-state-centralize-world-religion-at-jerusalem), they constructed a **worldview**. The [fixed calendar](/timeline?event=evt-year-of-our-lord-deception-1053-year-shift-forward) was part of broader campaign: **uniformitarianism** — belief that present processes always operated at same rates. The Sun rises. It sets. It *always* did this. There were never planet-gods. Never a [Golden Age](/timeline?event=evt-the-golden-age). The cosmos is, and has always been, *constant*.

## Constantine and Constantinople: Names Are the Program

**Constantine** derives from Latin *constans* (steadfast, unchanging), from *con-* + *stāns* ("to stand"): **"the one who stands unchanged."** **Constantinople** = "City of the Unchanging One."

The argument is not that name was invented for this, but **chosen** for it. In tradition where [titles were transferred](/timeline?event=evt-the-sun-replaces-mars-as-the-new-saviour) to match current theological program, it would be remarkable if most important city in new Sun religion received its name by accident.

Constantine worshipped [Sol Invictus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sol_Invictus) — Unconquered Sun — before supposed conversion. Sol Invictus was itself assertion of constancy: sun that **cannot be conquered, cannot be replaced, stands forever**. Transition to Christian Sun-Christ was rebranding, not conversion.

## Why Constancy Was Necessary

In centuries after [Mars](/timeline?event=evt-mars-aka-prometheus-replaces-venus-as-the-new-savi) departed, living memory of planet-gods was fading but had not disappeared. If people remembered [chain of saviours](/timeline?event=evt-the-sun-replaces-mars-as-the-new-saviour) — Saturn replaced by Jupiter, Jupiter by Venus, Venus by Mars, Mars by Sun — they would expect Sun also to be replaced, and current priesthood with it.

Solution: **deny the chain entirely**. Sun was not latest in succession — it was *eternal*. Cosmos did not change. And priesthood administering this eternal order was itself eternal, stretching back into increasingly vast fabricated past.

4. **Deep Time** — Extending timescale to billions of years served same purpose: making question of recently-arrived Sun absurd.
