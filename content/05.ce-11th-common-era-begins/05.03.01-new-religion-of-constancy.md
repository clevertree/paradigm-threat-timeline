## The Doctrine of Constancy

When Deep State [rebuilt Jerusalem at Istanbul](/timeline/evt-deep-state-centralize-world-religion-at-jerusalem), they constructed a **worldview**. The [fixed calendar](/timeline/evt-year-of-our-lord-deception-1053-year-shift-forward) was part of a broader campaign: **uniformitarianism** — belief that present processes always operated at same rates. The Sun rises. It sets. It *always* did this. There were never planet-gods. Never a [Golden Age](/timeline/evt-the-golden-age). The cosmos is, and has always been, *constant*.

## Constantine and Constantinople: Names Are the Program

**Constantine** derives from Latin *constans* (steadfast, unchanging): **"the one who stands unchanged."** **Constantinople** = "City of the Unchanging One."

The name was **chosen** for this purpose. In a tradition where [titles were transferred](/timeline/evt-the-sun-replaces-mars-as-the-new-saviour) to match the current theological programme, it would be remarkable if the most important city in the new Sun religion received its name by accident.

Constantine worshipped [Sol Invictus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sol_Invictus) — Unconquered Sun — before his supposed conversion. Sol Invictus was itself an assertion of constancy: a sun that **cannot be conquered, stands forever**. Transition to Christian Sun-Christ was rebranding, not conversion.

## Why Constancy Was Necessary

In the centuries after [Mars](/timeline/evt-mars-aka-prometheus-replaces-venus-as-the-new-savi) departed, living memory of planet-gods was fading but had not disappeared. If people remembered the [chain of saviours](/timeline/evt-the-sun-replaces-mars-as-the-new-saviour) — Saturn replaced by Jupiter, Jupiter by Venus, Venus by Mars, Mars by Sun — they would expect the Sun also to be replaced, and its priesthood with it.

Solution: **deny the chain entirely**. The Sun was not the latest in a succession — it was *eternal*. The cosmos did not change. And the priesthood administering this eternal order was itself eternal, stretching back into fabricated past.

2. **Suppression of Catastrophism** — Every memory of planetary encounters and plasma cataclysms redacted into myth, allegory, or madness.

Thornhill: *"As soon as they accepted global catastrophes in the dim past, the attitude should have changed to THE PRESENT IS NOT THE KEY TO THE PAST."*
4. **Deep Time** — Extending timescale to billions of years made the question of a recently-arrived Sun absurd.
