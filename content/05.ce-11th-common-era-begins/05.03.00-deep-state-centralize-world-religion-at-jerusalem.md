# Deep State Centralize World Religion at Jerusalem

![Deep State Centralizes World Religion](/media/05.ce-11th-common-era-begins/deep-state-centralizes-world-religion.png)

After roughly 822 years in exile, the Deep State resurfaced to rebuild their Monotheistic slave empire and claim sole authority to speak for the Creator God. They built a new headquarters at the narrowest part of the Bosphorus in north-west Istanbul — strategically advantageous. This metropolis was called Yoros in Turkish, Hierosolyma in Latin, and [Kiev / Tsar-Grad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tsargrad) (City of the Czar) in Cyrillic. In *redacted* Greek Mythology it was remembered as Troia or Troy. This was the holiest city on Earth because it was modeled after the Heavenly Jerusalem described in the [holy texts](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/06_07.html). This model *(6 gates and 3 walls)* was first seen as hexagons surrounding brown-dwarf Saturn in the Golden Age and is still seen [today](https://solarsystem.nasa.gov/missions/cassini/science/saturn/hexagon-in-motion/). In old versions of Jerusalem, each of the 3 *walls* had 6 gates, yielding **6-6-6** associated with [Satan](https://www.quora.com/Is-there-a-connection-between-Satan-and-Saturn) i.e. Saturn.

## Why Rebuild a Celestial City on Earth?

By the 11th century, [planetary saviours](/timeline/evt-the-sun-replaces-mars-as-the-new-saviour) were gone. The Sun was the new god: **you don't need priests anymore.** People were free — and knew it. (See [New Religion of Constancy](/timeline/evt-constancy-doctrine).)

No free civilization would have chosen to rebuild a replica of the old celestial slave-city. The 3 walls and 6 gates encoded [Saturn's hexagonal signature](/timeline/evt-atlantis-and-the-tree-of-knowledge). David Talbott confirms the creation myth's central subject was *"the construction of a celestial dwelling — a cosmic city, temple, or kingdom — revered across the millennia as the prototype of sacred space"* ([originsofmyth](https://saturniancosmology.org/)). Every earthly sacred city was built as a copy of this original.

The **only** group needing to recreate that geometry was a priestly class deriving authority from it — whose entire claim rested on being gatekeepers of the Saturnian order. The symbolic recreation was an attempt to **reassert control over a humanity that had just freed itself**. The Sun had liberated people from the priest class. Jerusalem was the answer: *we will rebuild the house of God on Earth, and you will need us to enter it.*

## The Fixed Calendar and the New Faith

The [fixed calendar](/timeline/evt-year-of-our-lord-deception-1053-year-shift-forward) provided the Deep State with a faith system based on constancy — that things change slowly or not at all. Jerusalem #1 in Istanbul was renamed [Constantinople](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constantinople) and became headquarters of the [New Religion of Constancy](/timeline/evt-constancy-doctrine). Since all information on the old Gods was kept secret in Jerusalem archives, common people gradually forgot them. Stories fell into mythology, replaced by accounts of the Sun traversing seasons, which later became the [farmer's almanac](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Almanac).

## The Crucifixion: Proof of Intent

What confirms Jerusalem as a Deep State control centre is what happened there later. The [Crucifixion](/timeline/evt-12th-century-ce-birth-of-christianity) was not piety but **political suppression**: public execution of a popular figure who threatened the priestly monopoly, carried out at the very site built to reassert that monopoly. A genuine holy city does not execute its prophets. A control centre does — when the prophet's message threatens the control.
