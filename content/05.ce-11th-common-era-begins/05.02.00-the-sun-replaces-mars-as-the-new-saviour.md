# The Sun replaces Mars as the new Saviour

The planetary saviour chain that began with Saturn closes here. With Mars gone from geosynchronous orbit, the sky fell quiet — the only remaining brilliant body was the Sun.

## The Sun as Final Saviour

With each planetary catastrophe, survivors rebuilt theology around the body that had just dominated and receded. When Mars departed around 670 BCE, a theological vacuum opened. The Sun — present throughout, unchanging, predictable — stepped into the role:

| Tradition | Solar Deity | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Egyptian | Ra / Aten | Solar disk as state worship |
| Greco-Roman | Apollo, Sol Invictus | Unconquerable sun as supreme |
| Persian | Ahura Mazda | Lord of light |
| Mesopotamian | Shamash | Judge/lawgiver (formerly Saturn's title) |
| Christian | Christ | Light of the World, solstice birth |
| Hindu | Surya, Savitr | Solar cycles as cosmic order |
| Aztec | Tonatiuh | The Fifth Sun, current age |

## The Sun's Dual Nature

**The gift**: Consistent light, predictable seasons, a working calendar. After centuries of sky-chaos, regular solar cycles were genuinely miraculous.

**The terror**: Ancient cultures preserved memories of solar megaflares. Norse *Ragnarok* culminates in Sol being swallowed. Revelation describes the sun scorching men (16:8-9). Aztecs held the Fifth Sun would also end.

## The Deep State Response: Son of Saturn

The pattern: **Saturn = eternal Father; whatever currently saves humanity = the Son.** Every saviour was plugged back to Saturn. This is why Greek god family trees never properly add up — they **retrofit** each dominant deity into Saturn's heir:

> "I am the LORD your God... You shall have no other gods before me... for I, the LORD your God, am a jealous God"
> -<cite>Deuteronomy 6:14-16</cite>

- **Christ** is the Son of God (Yahweh/Saturn), the Light of the World
- **Thor** is the son of Odin (Saturn), wielding lightning
- **Horus** is the son of Osiris (Saturn), bearing the solar disk

None describe the Sun as the son of *Jupiter* or *Mars*. Intermediate saviours are quietly dropped.

### The Human-Sized Saviour

Solar theology produced its most audacious variant: **a human being declared Son of God.** This only worked because planetary memory had faded. By the Common Era, centuries had passed since Mars loomed or Venus blazed — the human-sized claim was the *only* version available once the sky had emptied.

> *"It was difficult to convince people that the Sun was the son of God when it was obviously not the distinctive, axially-placed golden ball they remembered from the Golden Age."*
> — Jno Cook, Saturnian Cosmology

## The Chain Closes

Each successive planet earned the title of saviour, then revealed itself as another source of destruction and was replaced. The irony: every time they installed a new jealous god, its successor exposed the previous one as fraud. Their strategy — claiming each saviour as Saturn's legitimate heir — guaranteed eventual exposure.

Whether the Sun is the final link, or another body will rise to replace it, Saturnian researchers leave deliberately open.
