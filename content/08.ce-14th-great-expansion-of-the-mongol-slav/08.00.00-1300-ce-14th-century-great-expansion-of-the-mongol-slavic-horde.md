# 14th Century C.E.: The Great Expansion — Triumph and Betrayal

The 14th century is the story of two empires inhabiting the same body. Externally, the Rus-Horde reached its zenith. Internally, the [court was already infiltrated](/content/07.ce-13th-the-russian-horde-tartarian-empire/07.01.00-a-second-golden-age.md) by forces the Crusade sought to destroy. By century's end, cracks erupted in civil war at the [Battle of Kulikovo](/content/08.ce-14th-great-expansion-of-the-mongol-slav/08.03.00-1380-ce-giants-are-defeated-at-the-battle-of-kulikovo.md).

## The External Triumph

By the late 13th century, **Georgiy Danilovich** (Grand Prince of Moscow) — recorded in western/eastern sources as **Genghis Khan** — united Vladimir and Suzdal Rus' under *Lord Novgorod the Great*. His brother **Ivan Danilovich Kalita** (recorded as *Batu Khan* and *Pope Innocent III*) continued expansion and, per Fomenko, **founded the Vatican and Rome** — *VATI-KAN* being Latinised *Batu Khan*.

![14th Century Great Expansion of the Mongol / Slavic Rus-Horde Empire](/media/08.ce-14th-great-expansion-of-the-mongol-slav/14th-great-expansion.png)

By mid-century Russian cavalry invaded Europe, Africa, and India. In Western sources the Rus-Horde appears as the **Habsburg Empire of the XIV–XVI cc.** — *Habsburg* possibly from Slavonic *HAB* (NEW) + *BURG* (CITY) = **Novgorod**. The Horde chose **Florence** as Italian capital; Imperial headquarters were established separately, forming Rome.

With expansion came monumental construction dated by Fomenko to the 14th–16th century:
- [Tartarian](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/features/2021-04-27/inside-architecture-s-wildest-conspiracy-theory) cathedrals across Europe and North America
- The **Great Wall of China** and **Hagia Sophia** (= Temple of Solomon / Czar-Grad)
- The **Great Sphinx** and **Pyramids** at Giza — geopolymer concrete by Cossack-Hordian Mamelukes
- Megalithic **Baalbek** and great pyramids of Central America

In Egypt, a cemetery was established near Giza to inter mummified Czar-Khans. Dry sand [preserved](http://www.historyshistories.com/egypt-burial-practices.html) bodies transported across the Mediterranean — the "River Styx." The *30 Egyptian pharaoh dynasties* are phantom reflections of 13th–16th century Russian czar-khans.

## The Hundred Years War Begins

The conventional [Hundred Years' War](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hundred_Years%27_War) (1337–1453) is, in New Chronology, Hordian pacification campaigns in Western Europe. Many vassal regions chafed under military *tagma* (blood-tribute) and taxation.

![The Hundred Years War Begins](/media/08.ce-14th-great-expansion-of-the-mongol-slav/14th-hundred-years-war-begins.png)

Fomenko notes multiplication of place-names during conquests caused conflicts to be recorded twice. The transfer of Russian-Turkic names (Cossack → Gascon; Tatar → Tartar provinces of France; *Rus* → Prussia, Persia, Paris) obscured origins. Coordinated resistance suggests an organised network fanning the flames — inseparable from the [religious schism](/content/08.ce-14th-great-expansion-of-the-mongol-slav/08.01.00-european-religious-schism-emerges.md).

The campaign dragged on a hundred years; conclusion belongs to the [15th century](/content/09.ce-15th-ottoman-conquest-of-europe/09.00.01-1453-ce-hundred-years-war-ends-fall-of-czar-grad.md), when the Fall of Czar-Grad (1453) marked both war's end and the Deep State's pivot from resistance to infiltration.

## The Worm in the Court

The [giants](/content/07.ce-13th-the-russian-horde-tartarian-empire/07.01.02-the-giants-of-the-rus-horde.md) who led the Horde ruled by legitimacy and physical authority but were poorly suited to court politics. Smaller humans staffing the court — scribes, translators, physicians, priests — excelled at intrigue. Former Deep State priests who survived the Crusade wormed into the court as advisors. Fomenko notes Georgiy Danilovich (Genghis Khan) was **"murdered by conspirators"** early in his reign.

The full bloodline compromise — through strategic marriage, the [Heresy of the Judaizers](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/06_01.html), and [Esther = Elena Voloshanka](/content/10.ce-16th-reformation-and-inquisition/10.05.00-1552-ce-khazar-rebellion-in-the-russia-horde-empire.md) — came in the XVI century. But groundwork was laid here.
