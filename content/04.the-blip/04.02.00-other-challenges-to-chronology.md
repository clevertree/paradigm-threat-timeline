# Other Challenges to Chronology

Multiple researchers identify phantom years: Illig ~297, Heinsohn ~700, Velikovsky ~500, Fomenko ~1,053. This project adopts 1,723 years (670 BCE → 1053 CE). For gap comparison, Year Zero frameworks, and the Julian calendar proof: [Other Challenges to Chronology (Extended)](/timeline/evt-other-challenges-to-chronology-extended) in the Appendix.
