# Building the New Chronology

The Scaligerian chronology was challenged over four centuries by independent scholars — De Arcilla, Newton, Hardouin, Morozov, Fomenko — all concluding the conventional timeline is fabricated from displaced medieval sources. For the full survey and Fomenko's three methods: [Building the New Chronology (Extended)](/timeline/evt-building-the-new-chronology-extended) in the Appendix.
