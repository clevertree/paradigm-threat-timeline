# Atlantis and the Tree of Knowledge

![Atlantis - First Jerusalem built on the north poles of Mars and Earth](/media/02.the-golden-age/atlantis_first_jerusalem.png)
*Atlantis was the first holy city — First Jerusalem — built on the north poles of Mars and Earth to harness the energy of the Collinear Configuration.*

The planets in the northern configuration created a **Tree of Life** display directly above [Atlantis](https://zapatopi.net/blog/?post=201407156476.the_lost_continent_of_the_arctic) and its three cities **Og**, **Aryan**, and the 'Emerald City' of **Poseida**. Poseida, the capital, existed as a massive pyramid built from [grown emeralds](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emerald#Synthetic_emerald) — maximizing conductivity of cosmic energy. Its rulers jealously guarded their inventions until the city became known as the **Tree of Knowledge**. After the fall, these elites escaped with their knowledge, founding the **Pyramidal Empire**.

## Plato's Description Cannot Be Located on Earth

The only ancient account comes from Plato's *Timaeus* and *Critias* (c. 360 BCE). Read literally, his description is geographically impossible for present Earth — but consistent with a city at the **north pole of Mars**.

**"Larger than Libya and Asia combined"** (*Timaeus* 24e–25a). No terrestrial continent satisfies this. In the Saturnian framework, Mars was nearly as large as Earth and accessible from Earth's north pole via the plasma column.

**"Ships and voyages were not yet"** (*Critias* 113e–114a). If Atlantis couldn't be reached by ship, it wasn't mid-Atlantic. Plato describes a place reached by the **Bifröst** — the natural plasma bridge along the planetary column.

**Concentric rings "sheltered from the north"** (*Critias* 113d–116a). A city around a north pole with the continent falling southward matches the **Martian north polar cap**. Orichalcum, a metal "dug out in many parts of the island," has never been identified on Earth — consistent with Martian surface mineralogy.

**Repeated catastrophes** (*Critias* 111–112). Egyptian priests confirmed cyclical destruction: Atlantis was destroyed during Saturn-system collapse (c. 3147 BCE) and the Flood accompanying Mars' orbital departure.

## The First Jews of Atlantis (First Jerusalem)

The term 'Jew' originally referred to anyone worshiping Saturn as Creator God (YHWH, El, Elohim, Ba'al). The priest class claimed authority over all aspects of life, forming the protective outer layer of the **Pyramidal Empire** throughout history.

By keeping strict lineage separation, they passed down inherited authority over centuries. They appear to have no origin because their first appearance was before Atlantis's fall, when they exchanged advanced technology for obedience. Abrahamic religions remember this as the Garden of Eden and the [Tree of Knowledge](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tree_of_the_knowledge_of_good_and_evil).
