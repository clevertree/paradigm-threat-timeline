# 1196 CE — The First Crusade & Trojan War

## Revenge for the Crucifixion (~1196 CE)

The humiliation of the Crucifixion triggered a violent reaction across the gathering Rus-Horde. Within eleven years the Russians launched the First Crusade to recapture the Holy Sepulcher and avenge Christ.

Fomenko [notes](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/02.html) that medieval Church sources assert *"immediately after Jesus' Resurrection, Caesar's army went on a Crusade to Jerusalem and seized it"* — dismissed by Scaligerian historians as "vapid conjecture" because no Roman Crusade occurred in the first century. In the New Chronology, the sources are correct: they describe the late 12th–early 13th century campaigns. The so-called First Crusade, today dated to 1096, is correctly dated to ~1196. Fomenko stresses: *"the Crusaders were convinced they were avenging not the descendants of the Savior's executioners, but these executioners themselves."*

Not all giants endorsed the Revenge Crusade. Older, wiser giants knew it was a trap and that two wrongs make no right. The young giants (up to 40 years old) marched before the full army was ready — this first wave is remembered as the [Children's Crusade](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Children%27s_Crusade) — and were killed or enslaved. The older giants then assumed command and drew more cautious plans.

## The Trojan War

As the Revenge Crusade deepened, it turned religious: a new faith was forming around Christ and his mother Mary, while the Trojan side worshipped the ancient Deep State goddess Aphrodite. Hence the tradition that the Trojan War was triggered "over a woman." A decade of fighting killed many giants. The [Iliad](https://literature.stackexchange.com/questions/17439/what-does-branch-of-ares-mean-in-book-ii-of-the-iliad) traces some giant lineages back to planet Mars — the origin of the [Jotunn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J%C3%B6tunn).

## The First Great War

This was the first great war between giants representing Royal bloodline Christianity descending from Christ, and the Apostolic Christianity of normal-sized humans. The war concluded at the Battle of Troy/Jerusalem/TsarGrad with the giant Greeks winning. Unable to breach TsarGrad's walls, the giants constructed a giant-scaled wooden horse as a tribute-offering and feigned retreat. The Trojans brought the horse inside, unable to imagine that the giants had cultivated smaller-human allies willing to die on their behalf — loyalty born directly from the Crucifixion of Christ.

See: [13th Century: The Russian Horde Empire](/timeline/evt-13th-century-ce-the-russian-horde-tartarian-empire)

These original Crusades — the Horde expanding *westward* into Europe — are the mirror image of what came later. Beginning in 1812, Europe would launch its own crusades in the *reverse* direction: eastward into Russia. We call these the **Reverse Crusades** — see [Reverse Crusade I (1812)](/timeline/evt-napoleon-invades-russia), followed by Reverse Crusade II (1914), III (1941), and IV (ongoing).
