# Historical Christ is Born in Crimea

Christ's mother Katya was one of those [attempting to flee the census](/timeline/evt-hairy-mary). As a [hairy giant](/timeline/evt-hairy-mary) from Rus', she knew the census was not a count but a prelude to genocide. She made it as far as Crimea before being caught. The locals took pity as she laboured through a difficult delivery. The language barrier prevented them from establishing who the father was — many assumed she was a prostitute, or a virgin. Her husband may have been killed by smaller humans, and she *refused to speak of it*. Unable to fit inside buildings, she was placed in a stable where locals performed a cesarean section.

## The Cesarean Section and "Perpetual Virginity"

Fomenko demonstrates that "caesarean" derives from Christ's birth. In Church Slavonic liturgy, the irmos reads: *"Him, who before the Sun shined, and in flesh came from virgin's side, infinitely incarnated, we praise You, Mother of God."* The words [describe a cesarean section](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/02.html). Greek PARTHENOS (virgin) originates from Slavonic POROT', meaning "to cut open." The claim Mary "remained a virgin after giving birth" is literally true under cesarean: the birth canal was never used.

According to [eastern chronicles](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/02_18.html), the procedure stabbed infant Christ while still in the womb, generating traditions of the [Holy Lance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holy_Lance) and [Holy Grail](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holy_Grail). This birth wound may have been conflated with the lance of the Roman soldier at the Crucifixion.

Eventually authorities agreed to let the giants live, provided they accepted Baptism into Monotheism.

## The Magi: Rus-Horde Rulers Accept Christ

In Christ's epoch, Rus' adopted Christianity wholly and instantly. Fomenko [identifies the Magi](https://chronologia.org/en/how_it_was/02.html) as three Rus-Horde rulers: Russian Czar Vladimir (Balthazar), Czaritsa Malka — his mother (Melchior), and Cossack Ataman (Caspar). A Cologne Cathedral was erected in their honour, housing the Shrine of the Three Kings. Medieval images depict the adoration against banners displaying *a crescent and star* — symbol of Czar-Grad (Jerusalem). In the Cologne Shrine, two Magi are bearded men, but the third is *clearly female* — Queen Malka, later masculinised by Western artists uncomfortable with Slavic origins.

## Christ's Education and Conflict with the Church

Christ traveled with his family to Jerusalem in Istanbul, where he trained as a Rabbi. Rabbis endlessly deliberated to reconcile ancient Monotheism with the polytheistic religions they were absorbing. This backfired: Christ and his mother remained unconvinced of Church authority, deferring to her eastern Rus' heritage. Due to Christ's [clerical criticisms](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cleansing_of_the_Temple), his family was driven from Jerusalem. Matthew's Gospel preserves a redacted version in the [Holy Family's flight](https://www.memphistours.com/Egypt/WikiTravel/History-Egypt/wiki/Holy-Family-in-Egypt) from King Herod to Egypt — in eastern records, they were fleeing Istanbul persecution back to Rus'.

After returning home, Christ developed a following around hybridised teachings — parables demonstrating people were born free and needed no Church or God to mediate their lives. This spread quickly to Istanbul, challenging Monotheism's foundations. The Deep State laid plans to capture him. He was invited to a Passover celebration in Jerusalem, arrested, tried, and crucified. The charge was insurrection — not for challenging Monotheism, but for allegedly being *King of the Jews*, a title he never claimed.
