## Federal Money / Flexner Report — Energy in the Body

**Claim in transcript:** **Located** in *Recharge Your Battery and Heal*:

> ~1910: Andrew Carnegie and John D. Rockefeller became major investors in the pharmaceutical industry. The **Flexner Report** was written and taken to Congress. Congress **forbade the use of any federal money** for anyone who said there was energy in the body. That closed the **13 homeopathic medical schools**. Any physician using an **electronic device to treat patients got put in jail**. "That set up pretty much of a chilling effect and that's how our country stopped looking at energy in the body." Nixon went to China, saw acupuncture surgery, told NIH to make acupuncture part of traditional medicine — "but the people who control medicine... basically continued to ignore it."

**Clarification:** Tennant describes a *funding ban* and *criminalization* of electronic treatment (not a general research ban). The Flexner Report (1910) is real and did reshape medical education; its ties to Rockefeller funding and closure of homeopathic schools are documented. The claim that physicians were *jailed* for using electronic devices and that Congress explicitly forbade federal money for "energy in the body" **requires primary-source verification**.

**External research:** NCCAM/NCCIH now funds energy medicine. No current federal law prohibits such study. Historical accuracy of the jail/ban claim: open question.
