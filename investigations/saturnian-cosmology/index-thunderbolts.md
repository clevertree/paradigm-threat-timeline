## Overview

**The Thunderbolts Project** (T-Bolts Group Inc., 501(c)(3) non-profit, est. 2004) is the
collaborative hub of the **Electric Universe** movement. Its prime mission: explore the paradigm
that electromagnetic forces — not gravity alone — govern galaxies, stars, planets, weather, and
biological organisms.

> *"From the smallest particle to the largest galactic formation, a web of electrical circuitry
> connects and unifies all of nature… There are no isolated islands in an electric universe."*
> — David Talbott & Wallace Thornhill, *Thunderbolts of the Gods*

Key figures: **David Talbott** (founder; Saturn myth / mythology research), **Wallace Thornhill**
(electric universe physics), **Donald E. Scott** (electric sun / solar wind), **Ev Cochrane**
(comparative mythology), **Anthony Peratt** (plasma physics / rock art), **Michael Clarage**
(galactic dynamics), **Rens van der Sluijs** (plasma mythology).
