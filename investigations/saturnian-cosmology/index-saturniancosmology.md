## Overview

A research archive built around the **Saturnian Cosmology** hypothesis — the theory that,
in prehistory, Earth was a satellite of Saturn and experienced a radically different sky.
The site collects original essays, conference papers, and a massive library of third-party
reference texts organised by topic.
