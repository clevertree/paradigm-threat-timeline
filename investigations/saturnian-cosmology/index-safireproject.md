### Science

- **[science/phase-one.html](safireproject.com/www.safireproject.com/science/phase-one.html)**  
  **Phase One: Proof of Concept.** A fully functional bench-top model built before the full-scale lab. First iron composite anodes tested (2cm and 4cm). Key discovery: when plasma was first fired up, electromagnetic and radio frequencies showed a *rhythm — like a heartbeat, stable and continuous*. Observations of harmonic resonance possibly mediating a nuclear/fusion reaction. Quote: *"It's almost as if the universe is singing."* Full description available as downloadable PDF.

- **[science/phase-two.html](safireproject.com/www.safireproject.com/science/phase-two.html)**  
  **Phase Two: Full-Scale SAFIRE Laboratory.** The full-size SAFIRE Plasma Engine constructed and fired. All Phase One learnings applied. Entire lab designed in virtual space first (AutoDesk software) — 44,000 named parts. As a result, physical assembly took weeks instead of months and the system fired up and ran as predicted within hours. Montgomery Childs' OIPTS (Open Issues and Project Tracking System) methodology used. Phase Two went far beyond original plans — numerous unexpected discoveries including energy densities comparable to the Sun's photosphere, with no negative side effects (no radioactivity).

- **[science/phase-three.html](safireproject.com/www.safireproject.com/science/phase-three.html)**  
  **Phase Three: Advanced Experiments.** Experimental methods:
  - DOX (Design of Experiments Methodology)
  - Optical Spectroscopy
  - Mass Spectroscopy
  - Langmuir Probe Measurements
  - Plasma voltage and current control
  - Infrared and UV light measurements
  - RF measurement and data acquisition
  Full description available as downloadable PDF.

- **[science/commercialization.html](safireproject.com/www.safireproject.com/science/commercialization.html)**  
  **Commercialization.** SAFIRE PROJECT successfully completed its mandate to test the Electric Sun. Decision made to employ all discoveries in a commercial plasma reactor that produces no radioactive waste. Most recent discoveries suggest SAFIRE reactor will be able to *neutralize radioactive waste while simultaneously producing energy*. New enterprise: **Aureon Energy Ltd** → [aureon.ca](https://www.aureon.ca)

## Key Themes

- **Electric Sun Model** — the hypothesis that the Sun is powered by electrical plasma interactions, not internal thermonuclear fusion
- **Plasma double layers** — rotating translucent plasma spheres observed in SAFIRE; called "intense plasma flow discharge"
- **Harmonic resonance** — rhythm observed in plasma frequencies resembling a heartbeat; possible role in nuclear reactions
- **Energy without radioactivity** — SAFIRE generates energy densities comparable to the Sun's photosphere with no radioactive byproducts
- **Radioactive waste neutralization** — emerging evidence that SAFIRE can neutralize radioactive waste while producing energy
- **Falsification methodology** — designed to *disprove* the Electric Sun model, proceeding only if evidence forces confirmation
