## Overview

The English section of the **New Chronology** project by Russian mathematicians
**A.T. Fomenko** and **G.V. Nosovskiy**. The New Chronology argues that conventional
ancient and medieval history is misdated by centuries, and proposes a revised
timeline based on statistical and astronomical analysis.

## Key Themes

- Critique of Scaliger-Petavius chronology (Joseph Scaliger, Denis Pétau)
- Statistical analysis of historical texts and chronicles
- Astronomical dating of ancient events
- The "Mongol" Empire reinterpreted as Russia-Horde
- Romanov-era manipulation of Russian historical sources
- Role of foreign historians in the 18th-century Russian Academy of Sciences
- Previous critics of conventional chronology: Newton, Morozov, Velikovsky, et al.
- The financial and social structure of the Great Empire
