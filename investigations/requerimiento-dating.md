**Status:** Open

## Claim

The Requerimiento (Spanish legal declaration read to indigenous peoples before conquest) is conventionally dated to 1513. If it was redacted or backdated by ~150 years, its real adoption may fall in the mid-17th century or later, with implications for chronology of colonial law and narrative.

## Methodology

- Locate earliest surviving manuscripts or prints of the Requerimiento.
- Check whether any historian or alternate-chronology researcher has argued for a later date.
- Assess evidence for or against 1513 as authentic date of first use.
