## 12. Summary: Highest-Value Clues for Timeline

1. **Psychohistory = chronology manipulation** — Shortening 30,000 years to 1,000; planned crises; statistical method. Fomenko, phantom time, Scaligerian fabrication.
2. **Foundation as religion/priesthood** — Deliberately erected; priests control tech; "Galactic Spirit" invented; even priests believe. Deep State, Jesuit, Latin-as-control.
3. **Second Foundation at center** — "Other end" = social opposite = Trantor, not spatial. Library survives sack. Jerusalem/Istanbul, Rome.
4. **Conversion / mental control** — Mule Converts; Second Foundation Controls from birth; infiltrates opposition by joining it. Controlled opposition.
5. **Fifty martyrs / fake destruction** — Second Foundation stages own defeat; stays hidden. Revelation of the Method.
6. **Encyclopedia as cover** — Knowledge preservation as public narrative; real function is power. Latin, scriptoria, "correct" texts.
7. **Missionaries** — Foundation spreads via religious agents; "heathen"; territory control. Jesuit missions.
8. **Periphery vs center** — Empire weak at edges; center (Trantor) holds; periphery breaks away. Rus-Horde, Romanov, British Empire.
9. **Visi-Sonor / psychic probe** — Direct neural stimulation for audiovisual/emotional experience; mind-erasure. Plausible neuro-tech amid otherwise fantastic devices.
10. **Class / slavery / Marxism** — Ruling class, slave mines, "algebra of humanity," mass action. Materialist, deterministic historiography.
