## 7. Summary: Highest-Value Clues for Timeline

1. **"Let's get together" = trap** — Conference convenes humanoids for mass destruction. Institutional process as vector.
2. **We/They framing** — East/Reds/Soviets/Russians = They. Cold War as "game." Predictive programming of enemy image.
3. **Humanoid replacement** — Indistinguishable copies; kidnapped models; sent back in their place. Trust no one.
4. **Breckenridge = double agent** — Security insider is Them. Deep State infiltration.
5. **Russian robotics lead** — They ahead; Breckenridge had Russian materials. Asymmetric tech narrative.
6. **Panic as weapon** — Lynn: "moral and intellectual destruction... their being here at all"; "panic might do more harm than any one TC bomb." Psychological warfare.
