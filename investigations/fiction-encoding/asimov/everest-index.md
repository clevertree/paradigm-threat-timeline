## 6. Summary: Highest-Value Clues for Timeline

1. **Martians on Everest** — Non-terrestrial watchers; Everest as only habitable spot. Observation post for pre-space-age Earth.
2. **Tibetan "mountain of the gods"** — Indigenous taboo encodes truth; West dismisses until forced to look.
3. **Everest climbed twice** — Official ascent + real story. Dual chronology; hidden event.
4. **Planetary Survey** — "Real truth" vs public narrative. Institutional cover.
5. **Watchers worried about atomic/rocket age** — Timeline: 1953. Surveillance increases as human tech advances. Predictive programming.
