## 5. Summary: Highest-Value Clues for Timeline

1. **Discovery → instant interference** — Publicity triggers corporate, political, and criminal interest. Parallel: suppression of inconvenient knowledge.
2. **"Captain of industry" vs inventor** — Bankhead frames discovery as "ruining the country." Established interests control narrative.
3. **Multiple thieves, no protectors** — No law enforcement helps; patent "in Washington" is meaningless. Institutional capture.
4. **Nature as antagonist** — Blurb suggests natural forces join human interference. Encoded: discovery suppressed by "fate."
5. **Cheap gold substitute** — Ammonium oxide as monetary/industrial threat. Parallel: commodity control, value manipulation.
